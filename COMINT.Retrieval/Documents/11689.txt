setting others a lesson at pilkington. pioneer work with
    private viewdata systems carried out at pilkington brothers the
    leading flat glass manufacturer is discussed. the potential of
    viewdata, the choice of a system, its design and the uses to
    which it is put are described. its applications for a warehouse
    improvements system transmitting data on design problems around
    the world, for a control system for roller usage in the glass
    factories and for marketing information is explained. the
    future of the private viewdata system and other related
    technologies in the company are considered.
