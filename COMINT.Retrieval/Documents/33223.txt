price increases in natural science periodicals during the
    last five years. investigation was made into price changes
    during the last 5 years for 157 current natural science
    periodicals held by the university of ulm. while in the period
    1976-80 an average price increase of 5% was recorded, after
    preliminary investigations the increase rate for 1981 seems to
    be much higher compared with 1980; the average rise for 85
    periodicals appears to be about 21%. special attention was paid
    to literature documentation services which are relatively
    expensive to print. from 11 information services investigated
    the price increase during 1976-80 was about 8.2% per year;
    their price rise for 1981 has been announced as 13.2%. the
    possible consequences of these rises for librarians are
    discussed.
