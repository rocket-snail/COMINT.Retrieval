proceedings of the eighth annual canadian conference on
    information science' canadian association for information
    science' toronto, ontario, may 6-10, 1980. this volume
    comprises the president's message, the membership of the
    conference committee and sessions chairpersons, biographical
    notes on authors, and abstracts of papers (in english and
    french). full text of selected papers appears in microfiche
    (also included in a docket at the back of the book), whilst
