library and information services in finland. (english
    translation by william moore. the work is also available in
    swedish, german and russian.) comprises articles by specialists
    on various aspects of finnish librarianship: public libraries
    in finland, by hilkka orava; finnish scientific and research
    libraries, by martti blafield; technical information services
    in finnish, by pirkko innanen; economic life; and professional
    associations, by hilkka m. kauppi.
