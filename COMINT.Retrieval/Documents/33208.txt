archives and administrative change-some methods and
    approaches (part 4). (parts 1, 2, and 3 were published in
    archives and manuscripts 7 (3) aug 78, 7 (4) apr 79, and 8 (1)
    june 80 respectively.) deals with the effects of administrative
    change on 'record groups' and outlines methods of archival
    treatment of series which start under the aegis of one
    administration and continue when a 2nd administration is
    appointed.
