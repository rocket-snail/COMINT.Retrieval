a documentation francaise service' the political and
    current affairs information bank (b.i.p.a.). summary of a
    presentation delivered at the french librarians association
    public libraries group study session in jan 81. b.i.p.a. is a
    public archive for political, economic and social information.
    details are given of information collection and processing
    activities, in relation to administrative, political, press,
    and photographic information; and diffusion activities, with
    particular reference to on-line access to data, microfiche
    materials, and publications, including catalogues and
    indexes.
