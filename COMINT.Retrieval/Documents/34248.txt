user response to telidon. studies of user response to
    videotex may be aimed at 2 broad questions: 'how will the user
    respond to various design features of videotex?' and 'what will
    be the behavioural and social consequences of videotex?'
    studies of the response to design of various aspects of telidon
    have been conducted in the laboratories of the department of
    communications in ottawa. reviews experiments regarding user
    response to tree indexes on telidon, to reading from a telidon
    screen, and to graphics on telidon.
