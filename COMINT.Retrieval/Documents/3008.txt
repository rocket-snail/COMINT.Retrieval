an introduction to new media in japan. the captain
    interactive videotex and the teletext and telescan distributed
    videotex systems are described. interactive cable
    communications systems using conventional coaxial techniques
    and glass fibre technology and the video response system are
    discussed. the state of technical development at the japanese
    newspapers and their attitudes to communications developments
    are considered and the problems associated with the typesetting
    of japanese characters explained.
