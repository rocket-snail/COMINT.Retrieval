library education in israel. describes the history of
    library education in israel, as observed and recorded by one
    who has been active in part of its development, and notes the
    influence of foreign library traditions. concludes that in
    library education, as in many other respects, israel is a
    laboratory where the interplay of different (and often
    contradictory) social forces and diverse cultural traditions
    can be observed and, to some extent, tested.
