photocopying rights and wrongs' a librarian's view. paper
    presented at the 54th aslib annual conference, information '81'
    forecasts and realities, oxford university, uk, 22-25 sept 81.
    argues against the introduction of a licence for multiple
    photocopying as proposed by the whitford report of 1977 and
    criticises the committee of u.k. copyright owners on licensing
    of photocopying chaired by lord wolfenden. discusses the green
    paper 2reform of the law relating to copyright, designs and
    performers' protection' a consultative document 1(cmnd. 8302)
    and licensing proposals produced by uk publishers. presents
    alternatives to licensing of codes of practice and a copyright
    agency.
