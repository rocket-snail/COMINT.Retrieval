photocopying equipment).d iscusses some of the trends in
    the photocopier market, such as the growth in plain paper
    copiers with falling prices along with a reduction in size and
    weight and the inclusion of refinements due to solid state
    electronics and fibre optics. reviews the major processes used
    (xerographic, electrographic, thermographic and diazo) and
    deals briefly with factors governing the decision to purchase,
    rent or lease. features to consider when choosing a photocopier
    are listed and a glossary of terms used in the field is given.
    a report is presented on self-service control devices for
    photocopiers followed by test reports on 15
    photocopiers.
