cataloguing in publication' the role of the national
    library and the nigerian publishers. paper presented at a
    seminar on cataloguing-in-publication, held during the 4th ife
    book fair, 5 mar 79. outlines the meaning of a national
    cataloguing-in-publication programme; its advantages to the
    book trade, libraries and information centres, and the national
    bibliographic agency; and how such a programme could be
    operated in nigeria.
