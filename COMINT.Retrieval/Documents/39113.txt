serials in the '80s' a report from the field. paper
    originally presented as a speech prepared for the f.w. faxon
    company's management retreat held at stratton mountain, vermont
    in may 81. discusses the status of serials in us libraries,
    taking into account significant trends and concerns that relate
    to the larger library picture. considers' the role of serials
    nationally' new library concerns; the economic picture; the
    impact of library cooperation; the use of new technologies; and
    the role of the subscription agent.
