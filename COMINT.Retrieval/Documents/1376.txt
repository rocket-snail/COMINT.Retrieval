microfiche' an answer to a medical records paper problem.
    mcdonald health center, brigham young university began
    investigating the possibility of using microfiche to solve the
    problems of paper copy medical records in 1977. the 3 basic
    steps in the process; creation of the microfiche record, record
    retrieval process and archiving of records are described
    together with a full cost comparison between paper hard copy
    and microfiche based on the 1977 study.
