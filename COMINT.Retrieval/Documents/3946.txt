statement of policy relating to archives. a statement of
    policy relating to archives in the uk developed by the museums
    association, society of archivists and the library association,
    with an introduction by patrick j. boylan. the policy covers'
    the legal position of archive collections in museums and
    libraries; collecting responsibilities-general collections;
    special collections; other collecting agencies; purchase
    grants; disposal of collections; records management; and areas
    of positive collaboration between the 3 professions.
