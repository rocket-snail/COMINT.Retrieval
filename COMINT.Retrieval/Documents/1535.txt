cataloging administrators' views on cataloging education.
    a basic question in structuring a graduate library school
    programme cataloguing and classification course is the
    importance of teaching in the theoretical vs. practical mode or
    a combination of both. reports the results of a survey of
    cataloguers on their opinions of' (1) structure of the library
    school cataloguing curriculum; (2) relationship of the use of
    computers in cataloguing to the cataloguing curriculum; and (3)
    adequacy of preparation of current graduates for positions as
    cataloguers.
