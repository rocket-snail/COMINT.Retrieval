teleconferenced group decision making' designing for
    improved performance. (2 parts). this project is concerned with
    trying to get the best out of teleconferencing. it forms part
    of a broader programme of research by darpa to build a personal
    communication system to link members of the government
    executive and the military command at times of national
    emergency when it may be inopportune or inadvisable to have all
    the contributors to decision making in 1 place.
