information guides. a survey of subject guides to sources
    of information produced by library and information services in
    the united kingdom. reports the results of a survey of 1600
    library and information services in the uk which was undertaken
    by aslib in 1977 and was designed to investigate the production
    and form of brief subject guides to sources of information.
    factors influencing the decision to produce or not to produce
    such materials were also studied. the 530 guides found were
    analysed in terms of subject coverage, duplication of coverage,
    subject specificity, content, form of entry and layout,
    physical format, design features, style of presentation and
    written expression, page layout, use of annotations, and
    arrangement of contents.
