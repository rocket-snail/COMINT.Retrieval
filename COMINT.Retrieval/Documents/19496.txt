networks and automated systems in libraries in ontario.
    report of a visit to ontario to observe automated library
    systems in a variety of academic institutions. briefly
    discusses the operation of utlas and its on-line catalogue
    support system (catss) providing access to international
    sources of cataloguing data. dobis has been modified for use by
    the canadian institute of scientific and technical information,
    and cna-dobis is being developed in other institutions. an
    overview of the quality and extent of developments in automated
    library systems in canada is given.
