on the reader's own terms' literature for readers who
    have difficulty in understanding, difficulty in persisting, or
    who have another language. contribution to an issue devoted to
    library services for the disabled. discusses the swedish 'er'
    (easy to read) book system, instigated by a working group at
    the national swedish board of education. the books are designed
    for particular reader groups, primarily adult readers, who have
    great difficulties in reading or in understanding what they
    read. 100 or so titles have been published to date, and it is
    planned to publish a further 15-20 titles per year. a special
    experiment has been started involving the publication of er
    books for children and young people, although production of er
    books for adults is still a priority.
