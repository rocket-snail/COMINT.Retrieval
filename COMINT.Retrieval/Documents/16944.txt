classification literature in india' analysis and trend.
    an attempt has been made to analyse the classification
    literature produced in india from 1932-80. some of the facets
    considered are' type of literature (periodical,
    conference/seminar, book, thesis); periodicals publishing
    classification literature; productive authors; yearwise
    distribution of classification literature; and subject-wise
    analysis. the 160 depth schedules produced by ranganathan and
    his school are listed in chronological sequence. some
    conclusions are drawn based on the analysis.
