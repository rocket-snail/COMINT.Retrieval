university of london union list of serials. describes the
    circumstances which brought about the union list of serials
    project-the effect on collections of increasingly inadequate
    funds and the need for the rationalisation of the university's
    serials holdings. the objectives of the scheme are outlined,
    then the planning and systems design, which took place in
    1974-75, are traced. with planning complete, the structure and
    technical aspects of the system are described, followed by a
    description of input to the file. finally future plans are
    outlined.
