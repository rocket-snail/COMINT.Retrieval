a guide to dutch manuscripts relating to new netherland
    in united states repositories. intended to furnish researchers
    with the location of source material relating to new
    netherland, this guide describes the documents and collections
    held by united states repositories and provides accessibility
    to them by noting available printed or manuscript translations
    and indicating whether copies of the originals have been
    collected for the new york state library. the time period
    covered by the materials cited in the guide extends from the
    earliest years of dutch west india company activity in new
    netherland to the final relinquishing of control of the
    territory to the english in 1774; all documents are
    manuscripts, with one exception, and all are in dutch (except
    for some english notations and endorsements entered later).
