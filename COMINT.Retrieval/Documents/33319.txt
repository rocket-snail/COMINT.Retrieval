research into teleconferencing. video teleconferencing
    offers potential to reduce costs involved in bringing
    individuals from distant locations together for a conference. a
    teleconference system connects several sites by video, audio,
    and/or data channels. this report describes research by
    decisions and designs, inc., involving the design,
    construction, demonstration and evaluation of a video
    teleconference system that enhances the communication
    capability available with current systems.
