special libraries association' new york chapter. salary
    survey, 1979. this salary survey of special libraries
    throughout new york state presents earnings for librarians and
    associated personnel employed by corporate, academic,
    government agency, public, and other institutions as of jan 79.
    salary figures are analysed by occupational title, education
    achieved, tenure, and by numbers of persons supervised.
    includes information on working hours, promotions, bonuses,
    union membership, and fringe benefits. report not available
    from ntis.
