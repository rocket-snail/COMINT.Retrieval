chinese libraries during and after the cultural
    revolution. paper based primarily on data collected on a visit
    in 1978 to the people's republic of china. provides a brief
    account of the history of chinese libraries in contemporary
    china, and attempts to describe how they are meeting the
    challenges of a culture moving away from anti-intellectualism
    to scientific and technological researches, and what their
    salient problems are. the work is in 3 main sections' before
    the cultural revolution, 1949-1966; the cultural revolution,
    1966-1976; and after the cultural revolution, 1976-.
