dateline-washington. an account of a tour of the
    renovation work in progress at the folger shakespeare library,
    accompanied by the library's director, o.b. hardison. opened in
    1932 as a gift from the industrialist henry clay folger, the
    library contains material on and organises activities
    concerning shakespearean and english renaissance studies. the
    renovations include the construction of a rare book storage
    area and a new reading room.
