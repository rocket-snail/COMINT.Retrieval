ergonomics in librarianship. an examination of the
    science of the study of human beings in their working
    environment, in particular health, legal rights, and social
    ethics. the relevance to librarianship is demonstrated by 3
    problems' continuous activity in artificial light; exposure to
    the glare of the screens of visual display units; and working
    in conditions where temperature, humidity, ventilation, and
    dust control are inadequate.
