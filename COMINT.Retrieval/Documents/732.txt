the national library of the philippines. the national
    library of the philippines is a government agency, coming under
    the ministry of education and culture. established in 1900, the
    library is made up of 8 divisions, including the filipiniana
    and asia division, the public documents division, and the
    administrative division. its 3 reading rooms can accommodate
    approximately 532 readers each, and the auditorium seats 400.
    the bookstack capacity is 1,000 volumes, on 8 floor levels of
    stacks.
