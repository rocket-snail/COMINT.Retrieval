epie's programs and services' slj interviews ken komoski.
    reports an interview with ken komoski, executive director of
    educational products information exchange (epie), a non-profit,
    consumer supported, unbiased institute, which is chartered by
    the regents of new york state. describes briefly epie's
    services to educators and librarians, which include'
    information on consumer educational issues; evaluation of
    textbooks, audio-visual equipment, and non-profit materials;
    and in-service training. discusses some of the reasons why so
    many us librarians are unaware of epie's services.
