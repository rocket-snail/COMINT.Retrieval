education of the user of information. discusses
    developments in user education since 1926, when it 1st became a
    serious study, and pays particular attention to activities
    which followed the international acceptance of the concept of
    unisist in 1971. presents the findings of 2 surveys carried out
    to discover the extent to which college students and working
    scientists in pakistan received any formal library user
    education, and puts forward a plan for a national user
    education programme in that country.
