on-line acquisitions: use of a vendor system. revised
    version of a paper given to the ala rtsd pre-order and
    pre-cataloging bibliographic searching discussion group, 21 jan
    80. libris, baker and taylor's library information service, is
    an on-line book order system first made available to libraries
    in 1979. in mar 79, miami university libraries became 1 of 10
    pilot libraries testing libris. after 3 months' testing, it was
    decided to continue with the system, using it for on-line
    ordering with baker and taylor, generation of multiples to be
    mailed to other vendors, and preparation of form letters
    off-line.
