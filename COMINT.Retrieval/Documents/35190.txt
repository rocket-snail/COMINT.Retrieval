gateway-an enhancement of prestel. the introduction of
    the gateway facility in mar 82 will enable users to be linked
    via prestel and the packet switched service to external
    computers, allowing a range of new services to be offered.
    examines the signals coming from the viewdata industry and
    potential user organisations to analyse the directions in which
    this more sophisticated form of prestel may be channelled.
    outlines' the background to gateway's uk introduction; how the
    gateway facility operates; applications and the industries
    likely to be affected; and gateway's longer term
    influence.
