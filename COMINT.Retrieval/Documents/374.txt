audiovisual aids in the federal republic of germany. in
    the past 10 years audio-visual aids have become an essential
    part of the library supplies offered in west germany. at the
    same time the different aid media have developed in different
    directions. discusses the different aids available in sound
    recording media, video and slides and films. also mentions the
    services of the einkaufszentrale fur offentlich bibliotheken
    reviewing and reading departments and the difficulties of
    cataloguing sound recording media.
