pakistan librarianship 1974-1975. papers presented at the
    10th all-pakistan library conference, 31 aug-3 sept 75. the
    following papers were presented' development of the national
    library of pakistan, by a.h. akhtar; special libraries as data
    bases in the national information system network, by a.r.
    mohajir; problems of development of university libraries in
    pakistan, by najaf ali khan; development of college libraries,
    (a panel session); development of people's libraries, by mumtaz
    a. anwar; development of school libraries, by muhammad
    riaz.
