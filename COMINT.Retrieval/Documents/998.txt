newbery medal books are alive and well at court street
    school. describes the activities of the newbery book club at
    court street elementary school, lancaster, new york, which was
    set up to encourage readers at 6th grade level to choose a
    variety of good literature, particularly, newbery award books.
    discusses the way that the club has stimulated the love of
    reading and the use of the library media centre as a
    resource.
