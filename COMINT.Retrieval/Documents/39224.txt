prolegomena to the history of international
    librarianship. in the closing decades of the 20th century, the
    history of international librarianship is still largely
    uncharted territory. discusses evidence of interest in
    international librarianship (which is distinguished from
    comparative librarianship), the subject and scope of
    international librarianship, hypotheses underlying historical
    investigation, sources of the history of the field, an agenda
    for historical scholarship, and uses of the history of
    international librarianship.
