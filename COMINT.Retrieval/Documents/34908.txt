libraries in india-personal impressions). starting with
    the national library in calcutta outlines the structure,
    organisation, work, and the nature and extent of collections of
    major indian libraries. the central public libraries of delhi,
    bombay and madras and their networks of library services are
    followed by the university libraries of the 3 cities. libraries
    of technological institutes and important special libraries are
    also outlined. other aspects of the library life in india
    covered include the library association, founded in madras in
    1928, library law, library education, information services,
    bibliographical work, library work with children, and
    automation.
