use of microforms in caribbean libraries: a report to the
    acuril microfilm committee 1978 survey submitted to acuril
    standing committee on microfilm, november 5-10, 1978, barbados.
    a survey questionnaire was sent to all 81 member institutions
    of acuril (association of caribbean university research and
    institutional libraries) to determine their microform holdings
    and equipment. data is tabulated, with analysis. many
    collections are small but can be expected to grow, and over 1/3
    of respondents offer microforms on interlibrary loan.
