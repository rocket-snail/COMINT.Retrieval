arkansas public policy information system' arkansas
    econometric models. reports on the development of the arkansas
    econometric models as part of an overall description of the
    arkansas public policy information system (appis). appis is
    designed to be a prototype for the development of public policy
    information systems by other states. appis is a set of
    econometric models and data bases which specify private and
    public sectors in the arkansas economy. the system is designed
    to provide quarterly forecasts of state employment by 2-digit
    standard industrial classification, sources of personal income
    and detailed sources of state revenues.
