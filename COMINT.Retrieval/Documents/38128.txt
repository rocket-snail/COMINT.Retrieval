aacr2 and its impact on libraries' papers presented at

    conference on october 12, 1979, worthington, ohio. (edited by
    pauline r. bean and sharon g. fullerton.) papers are as
    follows' aacr 2' the 'new' code-how, why, and so what, by joan
    m. repp; aacr 2' planning for the change at the university of
