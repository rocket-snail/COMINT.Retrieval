excerpts from new york state education law, rules of the
    board of regents, and regulations of the commissioner of
    education pertaining to public and free association libraries,
    library systems, trustees and librarians. this collection of
    excerpts contains new york state educational legislation, board
    of regents regulations, and the commissioner of education's
    rules related to public libraries, library networks, boards of
    trustees, and library professionals. it includes all pertinent
    changes in educational law enacted through the october 1979
    legislative session and changes of regents rules and
    commissioner's regulations through the regents meeting in
    november 1979. report not available from ntis.
