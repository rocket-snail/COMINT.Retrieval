computer processing of librarianship and scientific
    information theses. describes the work undertaken by the chair
    of scientific information and librarianship at the faculty of
    philosophy, charles university in prague. for the computer
    processing of theses, system asti was applied. by producing
    varied types of indexes, this system enables a wide access to
    the stored data. its scope can be enlarged to include theses on
    a national scale.
