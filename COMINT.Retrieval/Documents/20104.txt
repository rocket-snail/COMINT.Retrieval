employee motivation' expectancy theory at work.
    expectancy theories of employee motivation have been the focus
    of much empirical research and theoretical writing. little
    attention has been given, however, to its practical application
    in the workplace. suggests that this may be because the model,
    as usually discussed, is highly complex and difficult to
    understand. translates expectancy theory into a form that is
    both understandable and usable by library
