army libraries and their role. surveys the role of polish
    army libraries which data from 1765 to the present day. the
    centralna biblioteka wojskowa (central army library) and the
    biblioteka naukowa sztaba generalnega (general staff
    educational library) and the other main army libraries are
    described. their roles vary but cover the following needs' army
    needs, display, technical information, socio-political
    information, medical data, naval data and history of war. the
    need to collect, conserve and catalogue information in books,
    periodicals and on film is stressed.
