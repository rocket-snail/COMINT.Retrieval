dissemination of new ideas in librarianship and
    information work. report of a seminar held by the british
    library on 16 october 1978. in oct 78 the british library held
    a seminar to discuss the dissemination of research results and
    of new practical ideas in librarianship and information work.
    representatives of the main national organisations involved in
    dissemination attended the seminar. this report contains a
    brief summary of the talks, an interpretative summary of the
    discussions, and a list of major suggestions arising from the
    seminar.
