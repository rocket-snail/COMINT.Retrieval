channel 2000' description and findings of a viewdata test
    conducted by oclc in columbus, ohio october-december 1980. a
    report of a project by the online computer library center
    (oclc), ohio, to test the feasibility of developing their own
    viewdata system. covers' the various facets of new consumer
    electronic information services, details of the channel 2000
    viewdata project-project initiation, conduct of the test,
    services offered, user equipment, the host computer and
    software; and findings and conclusions derived from the
    project.
