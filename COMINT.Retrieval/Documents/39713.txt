the abc of copyright. a primer giving an overall view of
    the essentials of copyright, attempting to provide practical
    answers to questions that are asked daily in this area. covers'
    copyright past and present; the moral right of authors;
    economic rights of authors; protected works; limits to
    copyright protection; copyright ownership; transfer of
    copyright; duration of protection; copyright formalities;
    infringement and remedies; international copyright; and
    copyright and the developing world.
