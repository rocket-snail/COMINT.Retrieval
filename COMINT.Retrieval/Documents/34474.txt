scientific information retrieval in developing countries.
    the centre de recherche retrospective de marseille has been
    dealing for 4 years with on-line searches oriented towards
    developing countries. north africa has been mainly concerned,
    and the experience of the centre built up in 2 ways' by doing
    works by mail exchange, the searches being conducted in
    marseille, or by going on the spot demonstrating and helping
    people to develop their own on-line centre.
