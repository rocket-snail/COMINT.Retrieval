education in medical documentation in west germany.
    describes education in medical documentation and information
    processing in west germany. there are 3 principal levels of
    training' (1) documentation assistant; (2) diploma (degree) in
    medical informatics, informatics (computing) with medical
    applications, or medicine with computer applications; and (3)
    certificate of the gmds, the highest accolade and a
    professional recognition conferred by a learned society.
