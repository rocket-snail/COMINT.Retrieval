evaluation of reference tools and other works. a text
    initially prepared to meet the needs of library students at the
    university of bordeaux, giving a clearly structured framework
    of analysis and evaluation. all essential points to be
    considered are ordered in a logical sequence of' purpose to be
    served by the work under examination; its physical
    characteristics; and intellectual characteristics, such as
    layout, detail of information, reliability, illustrations. the
    final section covers the evaluation of the work in relation to
    both the foregoing analysis and external factors such as
    feasibility of acquisition.
