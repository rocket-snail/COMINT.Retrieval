the library association/institute of information
    scientists special libraries and information units staffing
    survey 1980. the institute of information scientists (iis) and
    the library association (la) conducted a joint staffing and
    salaries survey in 1980. this replaced the iis annual
    remuneration survey and was part of the la's programme of such
    studies. 117 questionnaires were returned, each representing a
    special library or information unit in the uk. the results of
    the survey are reported briefly.
