aip en route to demand publishing. the development steps
    undertaken by the american institute of physics in their move
    towards a print on demand service for the journals and data
    base it publishes are described. these include changing to web
    offset with automatic folding and shrinkwrapping, in-house
    computerised composition, an atex computerised printing system
    and plans for digitised illustrations. a system for
    transmitting the full text of physics experiments for printing
    into laboratories' digitised data bases is to be developed and
    a low cost printout device is sought. the advantages and
    disadvantages of demand publishing are considered.
