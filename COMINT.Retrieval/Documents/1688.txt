microbiodeterioration of library materials. part 1,
    chapters 1-3. chapter 1 surveys literature on the microbial
    deterioration of library and archive materials. chapter 2
    describes general characteristics of various microorganisms
    causing this deterioration-bacteria, actinomycetes, fungi,
    yeasts, and algae. chapter 3 deals with the causes of
    microbiocorrosion of material by the metabolic products of
    microorganisms and the protection of such material by bacteri-,
    fungi-, and algicides. part 2 (chapters 4-5) and part 3
    (chapters 6-9) will be published in later issues.
