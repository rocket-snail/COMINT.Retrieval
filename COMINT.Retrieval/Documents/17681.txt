    reference department, including quick reference and
    bibliographic services, and special services including chair
    lifts and reading equipment for the handicapped, music studios
    containing pianos and media for listening to music, study
    carrels for language courses and microfilm readers, rooms for
    meetings and a cafeteria. the 12 subject departments are
    arranged in 4 galleries above the ground floor. their stocks
    include reference and lending material; reference and advisory
    servies of an outstandingly high quality are provided. there is
    a special collection of english language periodicals dating
    from the 18th century.
