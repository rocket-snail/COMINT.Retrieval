midwinter in reagan's washington: an ala conference
    report. report of the american library association midwinter
    meeting, washington, dc, 31 jan-5 feb 81. highlights the
    decision to study the concept of national divisional meetings;
    the passing of an amendment to the library bill of rights; the
    activities of the candidates for the 1982 ala presidency,
    norman horrocks and carol nemeyer; the meeting of attendees
    with congressional representatives; and tours to the library of
    congress and gallaudet college learning center.
