automated circulation planning for a region. in may 79,
    the mountain valley library system in california (mvls) engaged
    j. matthews and associates to prepare a feasibility study for
    the automation of circulation control activities systemwide. a
    number of circulation control alternatives were considered and
    2 options were examined in greater detail. a thorough
    cost/benefit analysis was made. an action plan for mvls was
    also developed to assist the member libraries during the next
    steps of automation. this is the final report of the
    study.
