the rules concerning translations in the european patent
    grant procedure. synopsis of the provisions of the european
    patent convention (epc) and the national laws of its
    contracting states regarding the stipulation of written
    translations in the european patent grant procedure. a
    distinction is drawn between translations which have to be
    filed with the european patent office and translations which
    may be required at certain stages of the procedure by the
    national patent authorities of the contracting states.
