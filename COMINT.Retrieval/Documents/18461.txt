planning for the adoption of aacr2 at the national
    university of singapore library (with special reference to the
    singapore/malaysia collection). paper presented at the las/ppm
    joint cataloguing seminar on aacr2, 8-10 dec 80. discusses the
    response of the national university of singapore library to the
    2nd edition of the 2anglo-american cataloguing rules1, and the
    need for more detailed planning before full implementation can
    be achieved. a quantitative study was carried out to determine
    the number of heading changes affecting the nusl's
    singapore/malaysia collection catalogue sample. describes the
    implications of the results of the study in the context of the
    retrospective catalogue conversion of the singapore/malaysia
    collection records into machine-readable form.
