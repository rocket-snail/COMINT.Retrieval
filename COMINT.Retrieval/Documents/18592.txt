the 2nd soviet-austrian seminar on librarianship. reports
    briefly on the proceedings of the 2nd soviet-austrian library
    seminar held in vienna 21-24 oct 80. soviet papers dealt with'
    principles of the library network organisation in the ussr,
    theoretical basis of the construction of the soviet library
    network and the estonian library network. the subjects of
    austrian contributions were' the system of austrian government
    research libraries and the organisation of a data base for
    periodical holdings of austrian libraries. the seminar provided
    an opportunity for soviet librarians to get acquainted with the
    work of austrian libraries. comments on library visits form the
    contents of the 2nd part of the report.
