dollar wise, pound foolish: british books in canada-a
    buyer's guide to some hard-won improvements. canadian book
    importing agents are moving to the point at which they will
    cease to buy british books, which are far too costly. the
    canadian book publishing industry is much stronger today, thus
    negating british publishers' assertations that only the price
    is a handicap to good trading. presents the comments and
    experiences of some of those involved in book buying in canada,
    and action taken in order to avoid simply 'importing
    inflation'.
