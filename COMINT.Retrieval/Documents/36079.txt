survey of new england hospital libraries. the new england
    library association's hospital library section sent a
    questionnaire to 472 local hospitals, with 159 usable
    responses. its original purpose was to provide hospital
    librarians with a profile of colleagues and to assist in
    general planning. questions concerned size and type of
    hospital; number, salary, and characteristics of staff; and
    library budget, collection, and services. the responses are
    tabulated and discussed.
