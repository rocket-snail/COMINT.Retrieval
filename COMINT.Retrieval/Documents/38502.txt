scientific and technical information services for
    socioeconomic development. volume 11. research paper. 3 papers
    written in preparation for the 1979 u.n. conference on science
    and technology for development. the 1st paper, by tefko
    saracevic, surveys the views on scientific and technical
    information expressed by developing countries and international
    organisations. the 2nd paper, by davis mccarn, surveys the
    information resources of the u.s., particularly from the view
    point of their relevance and availability to less developed
    countries. the last paper, by vladimir slamecka, summarises the
    dimensions involved in the use of scientific and technical
    information for advancing socioeconomic development.
