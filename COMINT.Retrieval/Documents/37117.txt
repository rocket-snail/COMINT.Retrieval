starting and managing a com catalog. report of the 2-day
    seminar starting and managing a com catalog' conversion and
    production, organised by the california library authority for
    systems and services and held at the university of southern
    california library school, los angeles, may 81. the 80
    delegates represented a wide range of libraries, mostly without
    com catalogues. topics covered by the speakers included
    management considerations for conversion to com; conversion
    options; preparing and living through conversion; quality
    control; format specifications; contracting; and equipment
    selection criteria.
