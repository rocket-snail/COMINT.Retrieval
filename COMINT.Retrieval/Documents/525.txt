processing for the young library user. offers advice to
    nigerian librarians on the cataloguing, classification, and
    physical processing of books so as to make them easily
    accessible to the young library user. recommends the
    compilation of a dictionary catalogue, and the use of a
    modified version of the dewey decimal classification system.
    discusses the type and number of catalogue entries required,
    and gives an example of such an entry. stresses the importance
    of the careful labeling of books and the correct filing of
    catalogue cards. recommends the use of filing rules such as
    those laid down in 2common sense cataloguing1 by esther j.
    piercy.
