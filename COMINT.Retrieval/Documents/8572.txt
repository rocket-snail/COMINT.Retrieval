computerized page makeup: just around the corner.
    important break-throughs in the technology of page make-up are
    imminent and will result in true computerised page make-up in
    the near future. identifies the advantages of this; outlines
    some systems already operating and 2 systems which enable the
    user to see 'typeset' pages without going through a typesetter;
    and identifies their disadvantages and limitations.
