guide to library skills. guide to the basic skills in
    using the library composed of 3 broad units. (1) orientation to
    the library, including computer-output microfilm, card
    catalogues, and location of materials; (2) reference books,
    including use of the dictionary, encyclopedias, and the
    vertical file; and (3) general indexes, including use of the
    periodical indexes, biographical indexes, and other sources.
