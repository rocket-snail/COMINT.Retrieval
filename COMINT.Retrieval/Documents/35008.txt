oclc-marc tapes and collection management. oclc-marc
    subscription service tapes can be used as a convenient source
    of statistical data for collection management. new acquisitions
    can be analysed by using monthly and annual cumulative tapes.
    information obtainable includes general statistics such as
    numbers of new titles added by subjects, types of materials,
    languages of the text, and intellectual levels. using boolean
    operators, it is possible to generate statistics such as the
    number of chemistry handbooks published after 1970, art history
    books written in the spanish language filmstrips treating
    american history suited for children, etc.
