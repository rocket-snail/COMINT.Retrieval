enguide. guide to bibliographic data bases for users of
    environmental information. lists' broad subject groupings and
    the corresponding on-line data bases and those data bases from
    which information is available on request; both types of data
    base (separately) with details of producer, scope, time span,
    size, types of indexes, and associated services; on-line data
    base suppliers; and relevant data bases for which incomplete or
    no information is available.
