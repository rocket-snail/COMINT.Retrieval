a review of public library development in asia. with
    reference to the author's visits to asian countries, discusses
    public library development in asia. emphasises the need for
    library legislation which has been introduced by only 6 of the
    41 countries in the region. the countries lack library
    standards (except for a few), adequate finances, physical
    facilities, and library resources. suggests action by national
    and international library associations and national
    governments.
