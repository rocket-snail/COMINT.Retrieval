an introduction to the educational resources information
    center (eric). overview of eric, the educational resources
    information center (formerly 'clearinghouse'.) central eric
    collects research reports from government sponsored educational
    research, state educational agencies, textbook publishers, and
    professional organisations. it also conducts and sponsors
    research, plans and directs activities of the system, and
    contracts for the other components. a clearinghouse was
    contracted for each of a number of major educational topics.
    reviews the abstracting and indexing services of eric and
    provides addresses of eric clearinghouses organised
    alphabetically by subject.
