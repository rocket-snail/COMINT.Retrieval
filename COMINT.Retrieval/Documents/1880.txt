library budgets: hard sell for a soft service.
    step-by-step guide to planned library budgeting, with
    particular reference to budgeting for ontario libraries. all
    ontario library boards are required by law to prepare a budget
    in accordance with the regulations established by the
    municipality in which they operate-examples are given of the
    budget situation in richmond hill, windsor and the regional
    municipality of waterloo. hints are given on avoiding 'budget
    buzzwords, and the relationship between a library's image and
    its ability to accurately develop its programme around a
    community's real needs is discussed.
