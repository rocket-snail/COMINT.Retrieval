tafe it from here. proceedings of a seminar sponsored by
    the university and college libraries section, library
    association of australia, at the university of newcastle, 18-20
    aug 78. the aim was to discuss technical and further education
    (tafe) library problems, and to suggest solutions. papers are
    in 5 sections' introduction and overview; management;
    educational aspects; standards for tafe libraries; and
    conclusions and future recommendations.
