french government information policy and its instruments.
    3 short accounts by the chief state organisations involved in
    the development of french national information services: the
    interministerial mission on scientific and technical
    information (midist); the university agency for scientific and
    technical information and documentation (audist); and the
    ministry of industry. details are given of national policies
    and objectives, market requirements, services developed to
    date, and future plans.
