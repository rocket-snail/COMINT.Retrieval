british library staff visit china. report of a study
    tour, supported by the british council, which took place from
    1-14 june 81, at the invitation of the institute of scientific
    and technical information of china (istic). the aim of the
    visit was to learn about the library and information system in
    china, to look at possible forms of cooperation between the uk
    and china, and to look at possible forms of cooperation between
    the uk and china, and to examine the scope for extending the
    lending division's international photocopy service to china.
    peking, shanghai, wu-han and canton were visited.
