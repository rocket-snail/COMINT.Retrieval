word processing equipment and applications. january,
    1976-september, 1981 (citations from the paper and board,
    printing, and packaging industries research associations data
    base). word processing and kinds of equipment and applications
    are considered in this bibliography from global sources. topics
    discussed include how to cut office costs, trends in
    photo-typesetting, laser printers, ink jet printing, fully
    automated records management, and digital equipment in
    conjunction with word processing.
