online access to the world transindex file. a data base
    providing bibliographic information and the source for
    translations of scientific and technical literature from
    eastern europe and asia into western languages, world
    transindex includes the information which was published until
    1977 in the world index of scientific translations, transatom
    bulletin, and bulletin of translations. it was established in
    jan 78 using the pascal system of the centre national de la
    recherche scientifique (cnrs), which permits the publication of
    indexes by photocomposition as well as on-line searching. since
    1975, cnrs has had on-line access to its own file of
    translations; in 1978, searching was extended to the world
    transindex file. report not available from ntis.
