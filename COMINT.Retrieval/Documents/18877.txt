recodifying title 44, united states code' the national
    publications act of 1980. contribution to a thematic issue
    devoted to us federal government printing and publishing
    reform. the us government printing office has eluded for a
    number of years attempts to install modern management
    techniques for the printing, binding, and distribution of
    federal government publications. in 1978 a concerted effort at
    printing reform was initiated in congress, resulting in the
    national publications act of 1980. presents an overview of the
    general conditions which prompted the reform legislation, its
    preparation, and the substance of its provisions.
