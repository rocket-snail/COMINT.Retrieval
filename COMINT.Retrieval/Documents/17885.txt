on the air or on the shelf?. contribution to an issue
    devoted to educational broadcasting and libraries. examines
    what can be done to ensure the complementary relationship
    between librarians and educational broadcasters, including the
    possibility of deposit collections of bbc educational
    publications in designated libraries across the country,
    organisation of study groups using library's televisions, and
    the use of video and prestel. describes the work of camden
    public libraries in this sphere.
