special librarianship. a new reader. collection of 70
    mainly original readings on special librarianship although a
    few are reprints of articles published during the 1970s.
    contributions are arranged under 7 headings' the past, present
    and future of special libraries; the special librarian as an
    individual; the special librarian-managed and managing;
    host-organisation-related; public-relations-related;
    tool/format-related; the special librarian as information
    scientist.
