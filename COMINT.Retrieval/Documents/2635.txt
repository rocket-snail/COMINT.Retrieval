measuring the effectiveness of a library skills program.
    the impact of an introductory library skills course is
    evaluated through the use of a difference of means test. the
    purpose of the course is to provide a basic introduction to the
    efficient and effective use of academic libraries and their
    resources. each of 55 undergraduates was pre-tested to
    determine his/her entry level of expertise. the test and the
    course covered competency areas' terminology, card catalogue,
    subject heading, heading arrangement, reference materials,
    bibliographical information, choice of index and periodical
    indexes. report not available from ntis.
