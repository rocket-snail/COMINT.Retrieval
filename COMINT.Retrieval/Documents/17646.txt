a bibliography of little magazines in the arts in the
    u.s.a. a bibliography of us peripheral literary publications
    with a representative smattering of art work, artists'
    periodicals, institutionally-sponsored magazines, independently
    published alternative art publications, publications with local
    or regional significance, other-format materials, serials
    concerned with specific aspects of the contemporary art scene,
    and critical and historical periodicals. the publications are
    listed alphabetically by title (with descriptions); details of
    defunct periodicals are given; and a geographic listing and
    subject index are included.
