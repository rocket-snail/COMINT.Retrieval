the library documentation centre. the library
    documentation centre is part of the national library of canada
    public services branch. it was created in 1970 to provide
    literature support for the work of research and planning branch
    (now the library systems centre), to meet the need for a
    clearinghouse on canadian library research and development, and
    to serve libraries and individuals (in canada and abroad)
    interested in this subject. outlines the centre's collections,
    services, and publications, and stresses its heavy reliance on
    the cooperation of the canadian library community.
