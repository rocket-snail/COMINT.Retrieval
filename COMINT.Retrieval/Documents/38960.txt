the programmer/analyst. contribution to an issue devoted
    to information seeking patterns of selected professionals. jim
    mcgill is a programmer analyst for a firm which designs and
    sells computing systems. he was questioned about his
    information needs and information seeking patterns in the
    following areas' information on a subject outside the computer
    science field; past developments in computer science; current
    awareness; specific facts or data; theories; and procedures and
    techniques. for each instance, he was asked about the medium by
    which the information came to him, and about any problems he
    experienced in acquiring it.
