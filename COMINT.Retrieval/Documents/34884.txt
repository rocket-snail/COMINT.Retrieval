articles on library instruction in colleges and
    universities, 1876-1932. no. 143. emphasising journal
    literature from the late 1800s to the mid-1930s, this
    chronologically-arranged compilation annotates articles about
    library instruction in colleges, universities, and schools of
    teacher education in the us. provides access to secondary
    materials for historians and librarians interested in academic
    library development and, more specifically, the origins and
    growth of library instruction. the introduction specifies the
    criteria used in the selection of entries, and both author and
    subject indexes are provided. report not available from
    ntis.
