computer-aided indexing in the arts' the case for a
    thesaurus of art terms. paper delivered to the art libraries
    society of north america conference, san francisco, 22 feb 81.
    describes how subject indexing activities in art are being
    aided by the use of computers; notes the current state of
    disarray among art subject indexing systems; and suggests that
    the availability of a standardised vocabulary will provide an
    important adjunct to computerised indexing.
