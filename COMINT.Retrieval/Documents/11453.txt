new information technology-social aspects, usage and
    trends. following a definition of what is meant by information
    technology (it) and a brief mention of some of the applications
    to which it can be put, advantages and disadvantages of new it
    systems are reviewed. the implications for mass utilisation of
    the new technology are discussed and the necessity for
    education and training in its use is stressed. an overview of
    various videotex trials is given together with reactions of
    users. finally some trends in the use and application of it are
    noted and a call is made for additional studies to ascertain
    the actual use the public makes of the information it
    seeks.
