the combined school and public library in olney, texas.
    the dedication in june 79 of a new constructed library facility
    in olney, texas, brought together the collections of the school
    and public libraries, which had been administered as a single
    unit with one board, library coordinator, and budget, since
    1974. a study conducted to evaluate the coordinated library
    which existed from 1974-79 examined the operation of the
    library both from the administrative point of view and on a
    day-to-day basis, problems which arose and their solutions,
    community attitudes toward the library, and the advantages and
    disadvantages of such library services. report not available
    from ntis.
