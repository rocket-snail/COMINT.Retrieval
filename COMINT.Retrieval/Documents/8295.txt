organisation and control of local government
    documentation. looks at the difficulties which face librarians
    in the identification, location and acquisition of
    local-government documents. it seeks solutions to these
    problems with the objective of formulating possible
    improvements. the main part of the research consisted of a
    series of visits to planning departments of local authorities
    in yorkshire, humberside and cleveland. the purpose of these
    visits was to obtain first-hand knowledge of local-government
    practices in the publication of information. also examines the
    poor coverage of local-government documents by 5 selected
    abstracting and indexing services.
