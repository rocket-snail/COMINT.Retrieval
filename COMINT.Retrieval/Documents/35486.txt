analysis of the current periodicals collection in the
    library of the centre for data processing in postgraduate
    computer science (cpd/pgcc) at ufrs, brazil' methodology and
    results. report of a study designed to investigate frequency of
    use and the composition of the nuclear collection, then to
    establish interest profiles and criteria for weeding and
    acquisition of new titles. data was collected from library
    records and by interviews with the heads of research
    departments. in addition to realising the initial objectives,
    the survey also drew attention to a group of periodicals of
    questionable value, to be monitored for possible weeding.
    collaboration with the research workers was found very useful,
    and a policy of further cooperation between library and
    research staff initiated. the results of the study are
    presented in full in the appendix.
