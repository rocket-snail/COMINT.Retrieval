reconstruction plan of the medical library at gerle.
    development of the medical library at the nyiregyhaza county
    hospital has been planned to become an information supply basis
    and scientific workshop of the parent establishment. the
    present poor conditions are characterised by a 40-sq.m reading
    room, the free-shelf storage room is at the same time a study
    room. the number of potential readers is 250 physicians, 1,200
    nurses and other professionals. development plans relate to a
    better coverage of holdings; subject catalogue; introduction of
    sdi; and broader interlibrary loans.
