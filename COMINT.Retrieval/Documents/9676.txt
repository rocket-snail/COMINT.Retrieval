promoting the use of federal documents' an experimental
    current awareness service. discusses the effectiveness of an
    experimental current awareness service for promoting the use of
    federal documents. based on the results from a questionnaire,
    the use patterns of faculties in economics and political
    science are examined. the study analyses the current awareness
    service as a means for identifying documents and the impact of
    the service on the faculty. among the users of documents, the
    service was an aid to research, and increased their awareness
    of the abundance and scope of documents, as well as increasing
    their use of documents. (a copy of the questionnaire is
    available from the author upon request).
