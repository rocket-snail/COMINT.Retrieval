a brief history of the public libraries commission' a
    survey of 25 years. a revised version of a paper given to the
    music library association in boston, 3 mar 78, and, in a
    different form to the public libraries commission during the
    salzburg meeting of iaml, 2 july 78. summarises the development
    and achievements of the public libraries commission, which was
    established in 1954 as part of iaml. discusses briefly the work
    of the commission in promoting and supporting the development
    of public music libraries, and notes the contribution made by
    its 1st president, dr alfons ott.
