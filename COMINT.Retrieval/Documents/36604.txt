historical map research. briefly surveys the history of
    the study of maps, and notes some prominent names and
    organisations involved in this study, either currently or in
    the past. distinguishes between intrinsic research and
    extrinsic research, and examines the ways in which different
    groups of researchers use maps to obtain information. discusses
    the problems involved in locating maps needed by researchers,
    together with the role of libraries and librarians in
    historical research.
