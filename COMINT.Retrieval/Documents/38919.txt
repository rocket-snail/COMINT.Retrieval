children's literature collections in fifty-four colleges
    and universities' what they are and what they might be. (phd
    thesis-university of iowa.) study of the nature, selection
    policies, and range among children's literature collections in
    selected us colleges and universities as they now exist. the
    study assembles librarians' opinions of these collections about
    what an ideal academic collection should be, and collects
    experts' opinions of what would be optimum standards of
    operation with emphasis on the selection of such collections,
    thus moving towards the determination of criteria for
    evaluating collections.
