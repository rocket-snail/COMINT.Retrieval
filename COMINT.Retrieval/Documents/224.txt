the care and feeding of bibliographic data. edited
    version of the paper prepared as a result of the us library of
    congress network advisory committee meeting on the ownership
    and distribution of bibliographic data, spring 80. it is a
    working document, designed to elicit comments from the library
    and information services on the legal, political, and economic
    implications of exchanging and sharing bibliographic data in
    machine-readable form among a wide range of private and public
    organisations. issues raised by the possibility of such
    activities are listed in 3 categories-ownership/distribution of
    bibliographic data, relationships among members of the
    information community, and international flow of bibliographic
    data. initial conclusions and recommendations are given.
