computer-aided management of requests for photocopies
    received by a documentation centre' annual report and
    statistical interpretation of results. describes a method for
    studying, by computer, requests to information centres and
    libraries for technical literature. details of the requests,
    such as periodical title, date of publication, source of
    supply, language, etc. are stored, counted, ranked and compared
    to allow annual assessment to be made of factors influencing
    the spread between various types of requests.
