relevance of uap in a university library system in
    mexico. an outline is given of the background of the library
    system in a university with 1 of the largest student enrolments
    in the world (over 300,000 students). this system covers 164
    departmental libraries which provide service at different
    academic levels. it has a directorate-general of libraries
    which provides technical coordination for the system and a
    central library supporting the departmental libraries. an
    account is given of the programmes that have been developed in
    support of the library infrastructure and the universal
    availability of publications (uap), in particular the librunam
    automated system, training workshops and continuing education
    and research programmes.
