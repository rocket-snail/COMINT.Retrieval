new techniques in the teaching of online searching. an
    institute for library educators (seattle, washington, march
    20-24, 1978). exhibits and summaries of presentations. using
    data bases of the dialog system new teaching techniques and
    materials are presented to an audience of library educators.
    included are search demonstrations, teaching approaches, sample
    exercises and instructional projects, multi-date base searching
    methods, utilisation of search aids and off-line resources, the
    conduct of presearch interviews, information search tactics,
    and educational programme planing. report not available from
    ntis.
