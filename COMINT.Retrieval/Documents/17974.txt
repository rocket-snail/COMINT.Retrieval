the role of institutes for further education in the
    implementation of new technical and scientific methods. the
    role played by institutes for further education in the economic
    strategy of the ussr is demonstrated by the example of the
    educational programme of the institute for further education of
    information scientists. the main goal of the educational
    programme of this institute is to increase the effectiveness of
    the state scientific and technical information system. the
    educational programme is discussed with particular emphasis on
    implementation of advanced working methods and on the
    cooperation of lecturers with experts in various fields of
    science and technology.
