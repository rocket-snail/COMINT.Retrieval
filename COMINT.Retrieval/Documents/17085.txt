william c. milner and maritime libraries. describes the
    formative years of canada's maritime library association and
    its efforts to organise public libraries in the maritime states
    1918-24. concentrates on the work of its 2nd president william
    c. milner, who ultimately failed to establish libraries in nova
    scotia or new brunswick. reasons for the failure were economic
    and political circumstances though some reasons were due to
    milner himself.
