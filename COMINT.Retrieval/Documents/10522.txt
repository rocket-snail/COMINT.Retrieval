the african library association of south africa-its role
    on the black library scene. the african library association of
    south africa has been in existence for 17 years. during this
    period it has concerned itself with the promotion of the
    reading habit among blacks and with the upgrading of the
    quality of persons engaged in library work by means of vacation
    schools and seminars. it has made representations to local
    authorities and the government and all those concerned with the
    provision of library services for blacks, either to start
    library services where these were not in existence or to
    improve the existing ones for the benefit of the local
    inhabitants.
