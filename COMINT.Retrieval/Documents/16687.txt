an observational study of man-computer interaction using
    an online bibliographic information retrieval system. reports a
    preliminary investigation into the man-computer interaction
    aspects of on-line searching. it describes the methodology
    developed for observing and recording mci in the use of on-line
    information retrieval systems. a brief analysis of the data
    obtained is also given, together with an assessment of the
    extent to which the goal of effective mci is being
    achieved.
