publishing and bookselling in kenya' problems and
    prospects. (mls dissertation). reviews the development and
    current state of publishing and bookselling in kenya and
    highlights the necessity for a viable indigenous publishing
    industry. discusses further opportunities for the publishing
    industry and discusses major factors likely to widen the book
    market. discusses the role of bookshops and their distribution
    throughout the country; acquisition of stock, trade discounts,
    credit periods, currency conversion rates; and bookselling
    problems and prospects. on the basis of conclusions thus drawn,
    makes 21 proposals for change.
