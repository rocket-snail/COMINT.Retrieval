programming and services strategies for two-way tv and
    viewdata systems-the telephone vs cable tv (prospectus only). a
    market survey of 18 different types of programming and services
    for the home is presented. past, present and possible future
    methods of implementing 2-way communication are reviewed. the
    probable market acceptance of the various kinds of service is
    considered in detail. a survey of the suppliers involved is
    included and some recommendations are made for vendors.
    research and trials in this area are critically
    examined.
