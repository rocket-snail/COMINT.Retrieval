wisconsin statistics' a directory of sources. directory
    of wisconsin and federal information sources meant to provide
    the user with initial contacts and sources of a wide range of
    socioeconomic data. the materials described are available
    through the wisconsin library network. major resources for the
    documents include federal and state depository libraries,
    public libraries, and system headquarters libraries, as well as
    the libraries of various state agencies; however, any wisconsin
    library can obtain materials for the user. report not available
    from ntis.
