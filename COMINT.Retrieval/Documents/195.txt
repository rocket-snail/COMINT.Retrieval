developments in blaise/locas. paper presented at a
    one-day seminar on the university of london shared cataloguing
    system, 3 apr 81. review of current and likely future
    developments in blaise-locas, which include' the use of the
    blaise on-line system in the context of locas; use by locas
    users of the editor; amendment of ukmarc and lcmarc records,
    their creation and their deletion.
