the oriental collection of the hungarian academy of
    sciences. traces the development of the oriental collection of
    the hungarian academy of sciences library, which was called for
    in 1926, envisaged in 1949, and opened in 1951. the focus is on
    the turcology approach to eastern studies, a discipline which
    has grown in the course of examining hungarian language and
    history in relation to turkish and turkic languages and
    peoples.
