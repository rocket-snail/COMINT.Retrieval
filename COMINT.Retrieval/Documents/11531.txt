first international conference on automatic processing of
    art history data and documents' pisa, scuola normale superiore,
    4-7 sept 78. parts 1 and 2. 2-part transactions of the
    conference, organised by villa i tatti (harvard), universita
    degli studi di siena, scuola normale superiore (pisa),
    ministero per i beni culturali e ambientali, and cnuce. papers
    are published in their original languages (english, french,
    german and italian) and relate to all aspects of the automatic
    processing of art history data and documents, including
    automated cataloguing and indexing and art history on-line
    bibliographic data bases.
