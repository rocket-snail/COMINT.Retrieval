a case about libraries: brief of 'amicus curiae'. details
    the action taken by the long island library association
    coalition against the board of education of island trees, new
    york. the case arises from the action of elected members of the
    board in ordering the removal of 9 books from the shelves of
    the senior high school library, one book from the junior high
    school library and one from the senior high school
    curriculum.
