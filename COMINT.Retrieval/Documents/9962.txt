report on an overseas study visit to the usa, 29th
    october-6th november 1979. the visit had 3 main purposes: to
    present a paper at the 1979 human factors society meeting in
    boston; to discuss the library guiding manual on which the unit
    is currently working with experts in boston and washington; and
    to make contact with information design specialists in boston
    and washington in order to publicise the work of the unit and
    of the british library's information design group, and to learn
    about current activities in the usa in relation to information
    design.
