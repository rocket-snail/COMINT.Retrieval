university libraries in malaysia' a descriptive outlook.
    (mls dissertation.) outlines the malaysian educational
    environment and the general role of the library in the
    malaysian universities. describes the general background,
    collection, staffing, accommodation, services and facilities,
    and technical services of the libraries of the universities of
    agriculture, technology, and science, national university of
    malaysia, and university malaya. describes their cooperative
    activities. they are much better developed than other malaysian
    libraries, are controlled by professional librarians, are
    building their collections very quickly, and have developed
    rapidly in the areas of services and facilities. computerised
    systems are being introduced.
