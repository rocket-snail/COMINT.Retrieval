library career, process of changes, development
    tendencies. contribution to a thematic issue on library
    training. the accelerated development of culture, science and
    technology has resulted in a new type of library user. the user
    now requires not only library material, but also analysed,
    evaluated information. this in turn has given rise to new
    professional branches of librarianship and information science.
    summarises the essential elements which form and shape careers
    in this field.
