alternative modes for providing graduate education for
    librarianship in ohio. final report. reports the results of the
    graduate education for librarianship in ohio project's
    investigation into the need for and provision of graduate
    library education in the state. provides initial assessment of
    the 4 options studied and expresses the hope that this report
    will foster further development of alternative opportunities
    for such education throughout the state, especially those which
    maximise access to state residents conveniently and
    cost-effectively.
