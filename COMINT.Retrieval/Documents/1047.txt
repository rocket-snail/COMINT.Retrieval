ultra high frequency switches and filters discussion pp
    the design of filters and switches for the london birmingham
    television relay link is described the type of switch adopted
    uses resonant stub lines in shunt with the main transmission
    lines typical performance details are given the filters are of
    the band stop type and consist of stub lines composed of a
    series of nominal line sections shunted across the main
    transmission line design curves are given and typical filter
    attenuation curves shown
