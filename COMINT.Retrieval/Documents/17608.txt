an application of information theory for materials
    selection and collection evaluation. (phd thesis-case western
    reserve university). study of the problem of materials
    selection for a university library subject collection. an
    objective, quantitative method was tested which classified
    books in a sample science collection, and which was used to
    assess the collection and to aid in the selection decision.
    relatedness of documents was presented as a selection and
    assessment criterion. single-link clustering and the brillouin
    information measure were used.
