the future of videotex systems' a personal view. reviews
    the technology of viewdata, in europe and the uk, its brief
    history, present problems and possible future. reference is
    made to panda (prestel advanced network design architecture),
    prestel's gateway system and picture prestel. concludes that
    videotex on an international scale could develop in 2
    directions' local information provision by distributed data
    base networks in individual countries, and as a 1st entry port
    to international data bases and data banks.
