terminology of the social sciences: the term 'cognitive
    processes' in the thesauri of two discipline-based information
    systems. (phd thesis-case western reserve university, ohio).
    hierarchical charts showing the arrays under the term
    'cognitive processes' in the eric thesaurus and thesaurus of
    psychological index terms demonstrate that the term appears in
    different hierarchies in the 2 indexing languages. 3
    explanations are offered, discussed, and rejected. these
    results suggest the need for further similar studies.
