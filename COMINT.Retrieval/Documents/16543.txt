microcomputers' tools of the present and future. updated
    version of a presentation at the american association of school
    librarians conference, louisville, sept 80. an overview of the
    role of computers in education, covering some of the more
    popular microcomputer systems; the production of educational
    software by independent software companies, educational groups,
    and textbook publishers; the likely future effect on libraries;
    and the possibility of a computerised information network in
    every large city.
