position and development of special libraries within the
    librarianship of the gdr. a brief explanation of the term
    special library and its contents and problems which the special
    libraries are facing today. measures are indicated to overcome
    these difficulties' responsibility of state management organs
    for libraries of their special field and their concentration
    into networks, the establishment of special library networks
    and centralised special libraries with methodological and
    guiding functions; the cooperation in the regions and the
    organs of the library association of east germany to develop
    joint activities and continued professional training.
