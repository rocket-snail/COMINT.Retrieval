a look at community analysis: some myths and some
    realities. librarians can discover how to integrate the public
    library into community life through participation in community
    analysis. discusses the myths which surround community
    analysis-for example, that it is nothing new, takes too long
    and is not worth the investment. contends that the idea is not
    new, but the study techniques are, community analysis is not
    difficult to do, and that the investment is worthwhile.
