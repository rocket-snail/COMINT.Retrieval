recent trends in west german university library building
    planning. reviews west german university library buildings and
    describes 5 buildings completed since 1973 at' ruhr university
    of bochum; dortmund university; bielfeld university;
    westphalian wilhelms university of munster; and bremen
    university. some comparisons and contrasts are made between
    these buildings and others in west germany built or restored
    between 1945 and 1973.
