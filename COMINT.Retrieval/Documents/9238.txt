online interactive information retrieval services from
    the inis data base in hungary. an account of the 10-month
    experimental period of the on-line retrospective search service
    at the central technical library and documentation centre
    (omkdk) of hungary. presents a brief overview of the structure
    and services of the inis (international nuclear information
    system) data base. the main features and operation of
    interactive searching are described with an introduction to the
    stairs system used for searching the data base. gives an
    example of an on-line session.
