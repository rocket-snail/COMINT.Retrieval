coping with the cuts. the recent fall of the pound
    against the dollar has aggravated the savage decline in uk
    polytechnic and university income as a significant proportion
    of periodicals are of north american origin. criticises the
    tress braun index set up by the university grants commission to
    monitor price increases. suggests the following guidelines for
    librarians' never separate periodical commitment from monograph
    spending; never run a deficit; and always know what next year's
    budget will be; and be able to allow for unexpected price
    rises. looks at the guidelines in terms of 2 model case
    studies. librarians should compare prices of materials through
    different agents and suppliers to get the best deal.
