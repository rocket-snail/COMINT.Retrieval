the national portrait gallery archive and library,
    london. describes the archive and library's move to carlton
    house terrace, areas of material held by the archive, and its
    organisation. the role and scope of the library complements
    that of the archive. the archive and library are research tools
    for both staff and other researchers. the move has facilitated
    reorganisation of the library to make it more accessible.
    through various publishing ventures information in the archive
    has become more widely available.
