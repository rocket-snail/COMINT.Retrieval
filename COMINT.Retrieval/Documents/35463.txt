on-line data-base services markets (prospectus only). the
    structure of the on-line data base services industry is
    reviewed and future trends are considered. the kinds of data
    bases currently available, price trends and service offerings
    are examined. the roles of and relationship between producers,
    distributors and end-users are evaluated. the introduction of
    new electronic systems is considered in relation to their
    impact on on-line data base services. 10 year market forecasts
    are given.
