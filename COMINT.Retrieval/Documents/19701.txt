tymnet-the evolution of a computer communications
    network. presents the communication problem facing tymshare
    from its 1st day of operation in 1966-the need to economically
    connect a user's terminal to the timesharing host computer-and
    describes the sequence of solutions leading to the creation of
    tymnet as it exists today. also explores the refinements of
    tymnet's dynamic routing system and problems of growth.
