a remarkable mathematician on the activity of information
    and documentation' homage to grigore c. moisil. recalls the
    memory of the rumanian mathematician, logician and philosopher,
    grigore c. moisil, member of the academy of sciences of the
    socialist republic of rumania. pays homage to his activities in
    promoting and popularising science and technology, and
    describes his initiatives, activities and innovations in the
    promotion of information and documentation in rumania.
