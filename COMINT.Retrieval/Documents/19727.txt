information services in university libraries: with
    special reference to the libraries of the university of
    baghdad. (mls dissertation.) discusses methods of providing
    information and current awareness services in iraqi university
    libraries, and their applications to the university of baghdad.
    there is a need to create a new image of the library amongst
    baghdad university library users. suggests the need for
    research into the current use of baghdad libraries and the real
    information requirements of the university's staff and
    students.
