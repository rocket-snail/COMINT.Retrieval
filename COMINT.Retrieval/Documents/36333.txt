improving the effectiveness of libraries through
    improvements in the quality of working life. examines
    techniques for the redesign of work in academic libraries.
    traces the history of work system design and presents methods
    that adhere to quality of working life (qwl) principles.
    reviews the state of knowledge about and the application of
    these methods. by implementing these or similar advanced
    methods of organisation and work system design, academic
    libraries should be able to respond more effectively to rapid
    changes in user demand and improve the qwl of employees.
