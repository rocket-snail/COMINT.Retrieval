the networking of west virginia' a workshop summary.
    report on 2 west virginia networking workshops held in july and
    aug 80. the highlight of both meetings was a presentation by
    vinod chachra of virginia polytechnic institute and state
    university, who has been investigating the state of the art of
    library technology and its implications for the development of
    effective, efficient library systems in west virginia. the
    presentation is summarised in some detail.
