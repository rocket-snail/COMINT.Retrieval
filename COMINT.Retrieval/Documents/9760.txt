agricola. agricola (agricultural online access) is a
    family of data bases consisting of indexes to worldwide journal
    and monographic literature and us government reports on general
    agriculture and related subject fields. it includes citations
    to monographs, serial titles and journal articles selected from
    6,000 serial titles from 1970 to the present, and a file size
    of over 1.4 million records. it is prepared by technical
    information systems, science and education administration, us
    department of agriculture. describes its subfiles and a new
    videotape produced to outline its information systems and
    services.
