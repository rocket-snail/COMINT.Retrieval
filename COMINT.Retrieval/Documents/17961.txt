organizational profile 4. information service, mind
    (national association for mental health). mind was formed in
    1946 from the former national council for mental hygiene and
    other uk bodies. it has developed an information service
    designed to provide data on all aspects of mental health to
    patients and their relatives, and to professional workers and
    the public. data banks are maintained at mind's headquarters in
    london and in each of the regional offices in cardiff,
    sheffield, leeds, preston and gateshead. discusses the
    regulation of the data banks and other services offered by
    mind, and describes its usage.
