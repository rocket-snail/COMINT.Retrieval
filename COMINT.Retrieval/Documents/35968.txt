transfer of information for the agricultural and
    veterinary sectors in denmark. describes the structure and work
    of the danish centre for veterinary and agricultural
    documentation. the national agricultural advisory centre,
    aarhus, cooperates with the danish agricultural information and
    in-service training center (lik), tune, whose functions are
    outlined. also mentions local publication activities and the
    development of data banks and data bases by the agricultural
    edp centre (lec) and other organisations. language barriers
    have been overcome. briefly considers overlaps of data bases
    and concludes with future plans and the immediate needs for
    international cooperation.
