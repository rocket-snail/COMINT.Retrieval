bibliotekstjanst-a presentation. contribution to an issue
    devoted to public library services in sweden. bibliotekstjanst
    is the swedish library service, whose main task is to help
    streamline the swedish library system. it has been shown that
    almost all types of library work can be conducted more
    efficiently and rationally with the aid of centrally produced
    services and aids. bibliotekstjanst, as the central service
    organ, has thus acquired a very wide field of operation.
