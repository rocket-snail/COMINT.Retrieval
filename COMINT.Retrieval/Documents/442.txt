resources for organising a braille library. discusses the
    3 chief fields of activity in braille libraries' production of
    braille books and tapes, with details of the equipment and
    personnel required; cataloguing and classification operations,
    with suggestions for location and arrangement of collections;
    and user services, including both information and
    documentation, and provision of additional facilities for the
    visually handicapped. the necessity for training volunteer
    helpers is emphasised and their role described.
