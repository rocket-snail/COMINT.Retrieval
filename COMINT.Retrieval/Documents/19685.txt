experiences of the regional centre of scientific,
    technical and economic information in gdansk. presents a brief
    outline of the function of the regional (voivodship)
    information centres within poland's state information system,
    sinto (system informacji naukowej, technicznej i
    organizacyjnej); describes the work of such centres, with
    reference to the centre in gdansk; and lists the forms and
    methods of information services used in their work.
