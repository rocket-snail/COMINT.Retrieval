the growth in japanese library research. paper presented
    at the 47th ifla conference, leipzig, 17-22 aug 81. in 1977
    information science became a category in the japanese ministry
    of education, science and culture's grants-in-aid system of
    supporting academic research. library research is growing but
    still lags behind most other subject areas. shows the priority
    given to research into automation and more sophisticated
    cataloguing techniques by analysing articles published in
    2library and information science1 1975-79. the university of
    library and information science was established in 1980; other
    important centres for research are keio university and tokyo
    university.
