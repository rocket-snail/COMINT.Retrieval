purchasing for libraries and information centers. part
    iia-bids and contracts. for part 1 see 80/3195. library and
    information managers have become increasingly concerned about
    the fiscal management functions in their organisations as they
    have watched inflation erode the purchasing power of their
    budgets. staff members need to understand more managerial
    concepts, some of which are associated with the purchasing
    process. bids and contracts are considered to be 2 of the major
    elements of the purchasing process. provides a conceptual
    understanding of bids and contracts and a set of guidelines for
    competitive bidding and contracts.
