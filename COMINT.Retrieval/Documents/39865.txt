general criteria applied by the venezuelan banco del
    libro in selecting books for children and young people. the
    banco del libro, a private organisation concerned with
    libraries and educational publishing, draws up and distributes
    a list of recommended children's books. describes how the
    selection department obtains titles for submission to the
    selection committee, and discusses the points considered in
    evaluation' suitability for a particular age group; awareness
    of social and cultural realities; theme and style; literary
    quality; visual quality; layout and design; and attitudes and
    values represented. in conclusion the difficulties of
    publishing and distributing books in venezuela are briefly
    considered.
