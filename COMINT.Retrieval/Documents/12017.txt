modern business information 1. the 1st part of a 3-part
    survey, comprising the following' british telecom gets down to

    technology in the uk telecommunications industry); plugging
    into the network, by norman hoppe (outlines available
    electronic switching systems and their future in the uk); the

    communications (outlines remote and mobile systems of
    communication available in the uk); instant confirmation
    (outlines facsimile transmission facilities in the uk); and
    typing the message (describes modern telex facilities in the
    uk)-the last 3 articles are all by elizabeth hennessy.
