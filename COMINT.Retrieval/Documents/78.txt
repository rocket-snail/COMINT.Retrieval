the link between publishing and libraries in the union of
    soviet socialist republics. describes the various ways in which
    publishers and libraries work together in the soviet union,
    this being an important means of ensuring the universal
    availability of publications. the links between publishers and
    libraries include, organisations of library distributors,
    automated systems for the management of printing, the making-up
    of acquisition of stocks, cataloguing and standardisation,
    specialised publishing houses and joint conferences which are
    held regularly to discuss common problems.
