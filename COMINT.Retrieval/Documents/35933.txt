decision support systems in libraries. following a review
    of the contributions of computers and managerial
    science/operations research to the management of libraries,
    this paper introduces the concept of decision support systems.
    dss, a blending of these techniques, can lead to more effective
    decisions by library managers. a case study of the utilisation
    of a dss in the budgeting process of a medium sized library
    illustrates the process. report not available from ntis.
