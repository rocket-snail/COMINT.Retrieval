establishing an environmental information center' a guide
    to organization and operation. the center for environmental
    information (cei) was established to serve the rochester, ny
    metropolitan region as a clearing-house for environmental
    concerns. since its inception, cei has been developing into a
    public service science centre. presents detailed information of
    cei's (1) early history; (2) administration and management; (3)
    programmes and services; (4) membership and clientele; and (5)
    finances, budgets, and fund raising. the possibility of using
    computerised information support for a community-based centre
    such as cei is explored.
