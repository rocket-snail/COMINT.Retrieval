professionalism under attack. professions are defined as
    a way of organising work. the attack on professions is set
    within the context of world interdependence and conflict
    between capitalism and socialism. professions are a
    capitalistic form of organising work. sources of attack
    described are radicalism, egalitarianism, consumerism, and
    affirmative action. one source of power to counteract attack is
    the professional association.
