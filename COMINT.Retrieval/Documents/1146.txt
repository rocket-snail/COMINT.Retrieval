the information system of the czechoslovak federal
    assembly. the information system of the czechoslovak federal
    assembly consists of a library, an archive and a press office.
    its main task is to provide information to the mps through its
    book and periodical collections and through archival material
    containing documents concerning the development of the
    czechoslovak republic. basic information on the activities of
    the organs of the parliament is provided by a bulletin. the
    history and a description of the library, founded in 1918, are
    given in greater detail.
