special libraries and the copyright act 1968-1980. paper
    presented to the annual general meeting of the laa special
    libraries section, queensland group, 19 nov 80. outlines the
    changes to the copyright act which have an impact on the
    copying of books or periodicals. sections include: what is
    copyright? what copying does the act permit? copying by
    libraries for users; copying for preservation and other
    purposes; and offences and penalties. several examples of
    photocopying documentation from the south-east queensland
    electricity board, brisbane, are appended.
