a conversation with don roberts. part two. in the 2nd
    part of an oct 80 telephone interview (the 1st part was
    published in technicalities 1 (2) jan 81 (see 81/5014) don
    roberts discusses the paperless revolution, quantum leaps in
    technology, guerilla communications (use of big business
    apparatus such as telephone networks for alternative
    communication), the need to expand personal information
    networks, and the minds that are preceding and being affected
    by these changes.
