photography & its use in library work. photography
    has taken on an increasingly important role in public library
    work. most often thought of in terms of outreach work and
    publicity, its effect has been even more dramatic in the impact
    of microforms. among reader services the availability of
    photocopiers is already taken for granted and in the near
    future improved models capable of reproducing tone
    illustrations or instant copiers using polaroid film will be
    equally common. suggests some ideas and some methods for
    creative application of photography in library work, together
    with sufficient technical information to enable the
    non-photographer to put them into effect.
