the role of the persatuan perpustakaan malaysia (library
    association of malaysia) in providing professional leadership
    in malaysia. (ma dissertation). the persatuan perpustakaan
    malaysia (ppm) was inaugurated on 25 mar 55 as the malayan
    library group, and it marked the earliest attempt to form an
    association by librarians in malaysia (then malaya) and
    singapore. ppm has assumed the role of the leader of the
    malaysian library profession. examines the style of leadership,
    questioning its efficacy and professionalism. attempts to
    determine the strengths and weaknesses of the library
    profession vis-a-vis the role which ppm can play.
