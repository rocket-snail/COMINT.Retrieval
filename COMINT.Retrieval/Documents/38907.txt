denmark's school library association bookrack award 1981.
    the bookrack award for 1981 was awarded to aase bredsdorff, a
    former inspector of libraries for her outstanding work in
    making literature accessible to children and in promoting the
    association's aim of providing children and young people with
    access to experience and information through libraries. the
    person awarded the bookrack holds it for the year and receives
    a copy of the book nominated, which for 1981 was 2stemplerne1
    by john nehm.
