fringe benefits in large public libraries of the united
    states-the professional's guide to 1980. a summary of orlando
    (florida) public library's 1980 work benefits survey of public
    libraries with annual budgets of at least $1 million. 76
    libraries throughout the usa gave details of benefits available
    to full-time professional staff concerning hours of work, paid
    holidays, leave, sick leave, death leave, retirement, health
    insurance, life insurance, parking, and additional benefits.
    the survey is carried out annually.
