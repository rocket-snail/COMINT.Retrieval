speyer municipal library in new premises. in 1980 a large
    house built in the 1870s was adapted at a cost of 2.5 million
    dm to house speyer public library and adult education
    institute. the new library, at the 2nd level of the national
    network, stocks 50,000 items for a town of 43,000 inhabitants.
    it is open 24 hours per week and its budget for 1981 totalled
    70,000 dm. the ground floor contains the issue counter, adult
    fiction and non-fiction, periodicals, media centre,
    reading-room, offices and processing section. the 1st floor
    contains a spacious children's library; the special teenage
    section provides books, a chess corner and facilities for
    playing tapes and records. the top floor houses a meeting-room
    and more offices.
