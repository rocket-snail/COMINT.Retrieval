microfilming of technical drawings. paper presented at
    the seminar on microfilming technical drawings, maps and plans,
    18 oct 79. discusses the various formats that are used for
    producing microforms of technical drawings, the major one being
    the 35 mm aperture card. the equipment and processes used for
    filming, processing and mounting in-house are outlined.
    discussion is given to the advantages and disadvantages of the
    use of bureaux services for filming technical drawings.
