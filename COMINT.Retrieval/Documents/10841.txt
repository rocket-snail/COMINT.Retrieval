videocassette services in quebec public libraries' the
    present situation. reports the results of a small survey by
    interview in 1981 of videocassette services in quebec public
    libraries. details are given of the equipment available, number
    of libraries involved, the systems used, and choice of titles.
    a comparison is made with the u.s.a., and in conclusion
    expansion of this service as an integral part of library
    provision urged.
