becoming literate. final report of a research project
    'adult illiteracy and the socialization of adult illiterates'.
    the study tried to discover, through interviewing a random
    sample of adult literacy students and their tutors in the
    nottingham area, the history of their reading/writing
    difficulties, the impact of these difficulties on their careers
    at school, domestic and social life and any interactions
    between illiteracy and any other handicap or
    deprivation.
