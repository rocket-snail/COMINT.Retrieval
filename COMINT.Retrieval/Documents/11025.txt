the library of the percival david foundation of chinese
    art. the library of foundation forms a part of the gift made in
    1951 by the late sir percival david to the university of
    london. includes literature on chinese painting and porcelain,
    in european languages, chinese and japanese, also many
    catalogues and local histories and gazeteers of parts of china
    where there were known to be kilns operating as early as 10th
    century.
