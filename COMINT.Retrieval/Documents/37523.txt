videotex and libraries. the study explored the concept of
    interactive videotex (viewdata) in a library setting and the
    potential usefulness for the australian business community. it
    was funded jointly by the national library of australia and
    telecom australia and undertaken by insearch ltd. study also
    undertook an assessment of the usefulness of videotex services
    'in-house' as information sources for the national library, for
    public libraries and as a vehicle for the library's services
    and announcements.
