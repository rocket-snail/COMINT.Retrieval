towards a national research information service for
    tanzania. documents initiatives taken to establish scientific
    and technical information services in tanzania. the programme
    has as a short term goal the establishment of a national
    information service for research by the tanzania national
    scientific research council with the cooperation and assistance
    of the swedish royal institute of technology library. long term
    goals envision the establishment and utilisation of modern
    computer based methods. report not available from ntis.
