computer assistance in indexing with index. describes the
    compilation of indexes to 2 books using the program index on
    the mts (michigan terminal system) operating the ibm 378/3033
    computer. discusses the advantages of computer indexing, which
    include' speed in preparation; accuracy in transcription; and
    ease of transmission from 1 location to another. concludes,
    however, that it is an aid rather than a competitor to indexes,
    since an indexer must still make decisions as to cross
    referencing, subheads, and categories.
