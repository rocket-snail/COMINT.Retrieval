women's studies and library history: a review essay.
    reviews 3 bibliographies on us women's studies: women's
    studies: a recommended core bibliography by esther stineman
    (littleton, colorado: libraries unlimited, 1979); women in
    america: a guide to information sources edited by virginia r.
    torris (american studies information guide series, vol. 7)
    detroit: gale research company, 1980; and women's history
    sources: a guide to archives and manuscript collections in the
    united states, 2 vols. vol. 1 edited by andrea hinding, vol. 2
    edited by suzanna moody (new york: bowker, 1979).
