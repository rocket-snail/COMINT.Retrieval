the report of the librarians' union 1979-80. includes the
    accounts of the librarians' union for 1979, the executive
    committee's budget proposals for 1981, report of the
    librarians' union's action fund for 1979-80, report of the
    librarians' union for 1979-80, report of the union's group for
    state employed librarians 1979-80, report of the union's group
    for privately employed librarians 1979-80, and reports of the
    following special groups' the group for branches and mobile
    libraries, the group for children's and young people's
    librarians, the group for outreach library services, and the
    group 'library and education'.
