in case of unions, use this. contribution to an issue
    devoted to papers presented at the canadian library association
    annual conference, june 81. discusses the development of unions
    on canadian libraries, and advises library board members on
    dealing with unions. highlights the need for board members to
    be familiar with the requirements of their provinces's labour
    code, and for chief librarians and their assistants to be well
    trained in contract administration. also considers bargaining
    and negotiating tactics.
