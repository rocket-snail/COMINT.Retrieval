life and work of professor arntz. an fid tribute. paper
    based on an address delivered at the fid congress banquet, held
    in copenhagen 21 aug 80. highlights the contributions and
    achievements of professor h. arntz in the field of
    documentation and information science and traces his early
    career and interest in microfilms and reprography. reviews the
    activities of fid when professor arntz was president and pays
    tribute to his work and achievements within the
    federation.
