general aspects of technical and scientific information
    in research works done in health institutions. in the banska
    bystrica country health institute of slovakia library and
    information activities have been taking place for years.
    wishing to improve their services a survey by questionnaire was
    made to reveal user demand on literature. from the 250
    questionnaires sent out, 60 were answered. besides usual
    answers it deserves mention that 31.7% of respondents voted for
    books, 43.3% for periodicals. usually there is a bigger shift
    toward periodicals than in this survey.
