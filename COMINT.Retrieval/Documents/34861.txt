centralisation and treatment of mail, documentation,
    information and archives' the experience of the geneva
    department of public works. an account of the procedures
    adopted following the concentration of all services in one
    building. details are given of the system evolved for handling
    both current and special mail, specifying routines for dealing
    with communications from state departments; the tasks assigned
    to the documentation centre; and archive operations in relation
    to subject fields covered, showing how udc is used. archive
    criteria for conservation and destruction of documents, and use
    of microfilming, are described, and cataloguing techniques and
    equipment used for 16mm microfilming specified. relevant
    standard documentation relating to all aspects of the services
    is appended to illustrate the system.
