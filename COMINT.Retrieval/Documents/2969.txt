thanks to hansjorg suberkrub: the retirement of a great
    librarian. after the war suberkrub studied at kiel university
    and hamburg library school. he worked in kiel library from
    1954-55 and in the following year became municipal librarian of
    bielefeld, where he remained, modernising and expanding library
    services until his retirement in oct 81. suberkrub has been an
    active member of many regional, national and international
    committees, especially as chairman of the german library
    association from 1965 to 1973. he played an important part in
    the creation of bibliotheksplan '73, a scheme for developing
    german libraries at national levels. since 1973 he has been
    occupied with librarianship in north rhine westphalia, advanced
    training for librarians and as chairman of the study group on
    large municipal libraries. he is a prolific and important
    author on librarianship.
