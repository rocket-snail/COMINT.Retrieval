where does one begin the information work? (letters to a
    would-be information officer). 2nd in a series of letters to a
    would-be information officer (for the 1st see 81/5456).
    presents a 3-month work plan for organising an information
    service; explains the organisational principles of scientific
    information work; discusses user needs investigations, work
    with the reference collection, and publicity; and presents time
    quotas for information activities and formats for working
    documents.
