information systems for national development-the social
    relevance of information systems. extract from the 6th gabriel
    a. bernado memorial lecture, university of the philippines, mar
    79. considers the role played by information systems of all
    types in reaching national development goals, particularly in
    developing countries. devotes special attention to problems in
    building the information infrastructure and information
    policy-making in a constantly changing society, taking into
    account economic, social and political factors. the use of
    information in rural development is treated in detail as an
    example.
