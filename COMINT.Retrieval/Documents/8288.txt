resources for soviet, east european and slavonic studies
    in british libraries. records library collections in the uk
    likely to be of use for advanced study and research and
    describes their contents in sufficient detail for the enquirer
    to make an informed choice between them. subject coverage is
    all areas of the humanities and social sciences. geographical
    coverage is all territory occupied by the ussr, poland,
    czechoslovakia, east germany (since 1945), hungary, yugoslavia,
    rumania, bulgaria, and albania. for each collection, gives
    details of content, admission, interlibrary loans, facilities,
    and publications.
