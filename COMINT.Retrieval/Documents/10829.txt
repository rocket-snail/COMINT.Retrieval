literature survey on library building 1979. continuation
    of the bibliography published at intervals in this periodical
    since 1969. about 180 items are listed, including books,
    conference proceedings and periodical articles published
    1976-79. coverage is international with emphasis on german
    material. main sections are:- general material; bibliographies;
    special publications; draft plans and competitions; planning
    and technique; national and state libraries; university,
    college, school and special libraries; and public libraries.
    each entry includes author, title, periodical name with
    pagination where appropriate and detailed information on
    illustrations.
