the role of branch systems in the state system of
    scientific and technical information. based on a paper
    presented at the all-union meeting on the place of branch
    systems in the state system of scientific and technical
    information, apr 78. sectoral systems are components of the
    state system. describes their development; information
    services; the role of the central bodies in creating automated
    branch systems, in scientific-industrial publicity, and
    international information systems; and the attention being paid
    to raising the qualification levels of information
    personnel.
