the slippery market for videodiscs. the development
    costs, sales, prices and principle of operation of the rca
    selectavision videodisc player are compared with the pioneer
    and magnavox machines. competition from video-cassette
    recorders is discussed and the typical kind of customer for
    each is considered. the development of a rental market for
    cassettes and discs are described. japanese development of disc
    players and cassette systems is considered and their video
    colour cameras are discussed. whether rca will recover its
    development costs for the videodisc-player is also
    examined.
