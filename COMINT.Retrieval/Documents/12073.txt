the versatile terminal. describes the experiences of the
    industrial research laboratory of kodak ltd in finding and
    providing a computer terminal most suited to its very varied
    requirements. these requirements include bibliographic and
    scientific data searching and access to a number of worldwide
    computing services for scientific computing work. the provision
    of a few additional facilities over and above the minimum
    required has improved the search capabilities and increased
    terminal utilisation in the laboratory. illustrates by examples
    the use of the equipment, control of the terminal, and the
    training of laboratory staff. also includes a discussion on how
    and why the equipment installed was chosen. report not
    available from ntis.
