libraries for all: a world of books and their readers:
    papers presented at the ifla 50th anniversary world congress,
    brussels 1977. papers and discussion in english, french and
    german on various aspects of the impact of libraries and
    information services within the fields of the book world; the
    world of government, law and politics; the world of education
    and learning; business, commerce and industry; science and
    technology, and reading for pleasure.
