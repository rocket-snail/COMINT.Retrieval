the impact of automation on the staff and organization of
    a medium-sized academic library' a case study. a case study
    examining the general effects of automating operations in a
    medium sized academic library on both the staff and the
    organisation over a 10 year period reveals that the major
    impact has been the reappraisal of functional objectives and
    their accomplishment. in regard to personnel, there has been an
    overall saving in labour costs, and while it has decreased
    professional staff requirements, it has increased support staff
    positions, many of which require greater skill, experience, and
    training. report not available from ntis.
