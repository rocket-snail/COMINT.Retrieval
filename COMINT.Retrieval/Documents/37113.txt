bibliographic information support for the complex
    programme 'siberia'. in 1977 the sibirskoe otdelenie akademii
    nauk (siberian department of the academy of sciences) launched
    a major long term research programme entitled 'complex of
    natural resources in siberia'. results from the research will
    be used in planning the utilisation of siberia's natural
    resources. in support of the programme a bibliographic
    information system has been developed by the academy's
    libraries and information centres to assist scientists in their
    work on individual projects. types and forms of services
    provided within the system are described.
