new information technologies' some observations on what
    is in store for libraries. the combination of the newest
    developments in automation and telecommunications can be
    implemented to provide library and information workers with
    qualitatively different services than ever before. via
    telecommunication links, electronic mail, facsimile
    transmission, etc., remote access to a variety of information
    sources which in the past were the sole province of a major
    research library will be possible. some of the areas of library
    activities where the effects of such technological innovations
    will be prevalent are described.
