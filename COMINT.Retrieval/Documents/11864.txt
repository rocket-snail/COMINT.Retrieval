improvement of the structure of the unified information
    stock of a republic. note. an english translation of this
    article is published in scientific and technical information
    processing, (1) 1980, 42-51. a method is presented which makes
    it possible to improve the effectiveness of meeting users'
    demands for documentation. the method, which is based on
    optimisation of the structure of the existing
    documentation/information stock of a republic, treats the stock
    as an integrated system consisting of several sub-systems. the
    organisational and functional changes of the stock of libraries
    and information centres are minimal.
