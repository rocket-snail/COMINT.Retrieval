status report on the map cataloging project at the
    illinois state library. illinois state library began its map
    cataloguing project on 1 aug 80. all formerly uncatalogued
    sheet maps are being catalogued in marc map format and those
    already catalogued by various methods are being recatalogued,
    using oclc. because the state library is a regional government
    printing office depository, the federal maps are being
    classified according to the sudoc (superintendent of documents)
    system. all other maps are being classified by the library of
    congress g system. describes progress on the project and
    discusses problems encountered and their solution.
