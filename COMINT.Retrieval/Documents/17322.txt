applying aacr2 in papua new guinea libraries. reviews
    some of the debate of the past few years concerning the
    anglo-american cataloguing rules (2nd edition); explains some
    of the new related tools that have been introduced and accepted
    in libraries throughout the english-speaking world; stresses
    the importance of aacr 2 for papua new guinean librarians and
    library users; and urges all libraries (especially those with
    large and very specialised collections) to adopt aacr 2.
