college libraries in andhra pradesh, with special
    reference to osmania university area-a survey. a study of arts,
    science, and commerce college libraries in andhra pradesh,
    india, with special reference to the osmania university area,
    which has 84 affiliated colleges. data was collected through
    visits and a mailed questionnaire. analyses the data in terms
    of management, staff, finance, building and furniture,
    acquisition policy, processing, circulation, maintenance of
    collection, regulations, and statistics. makes 24 suggestions
    concerning standardisation.
