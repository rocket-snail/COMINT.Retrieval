methods for setting rental and discard prices. a recent
    review of rental collection administration at new zealand's
    hastings public library showed that income from renting books
    was not keeping pace with expenditure on the rental collection.
    suggests a new method for setting rental charges (based on
    recovery of purchase price in 16 issues) and presents a method
    of working out the discard price of library materials (on the
    basis that they lose a certain proportion of their value with
    each circulation).
