a survey of children's services in ohio public libraries
    1979. survey carried out by the ohio library association
    service to children and young teens division. questionnaires
    were sent to libraries with no listed branches, children's
    service coordinators, branch libraries, and public libraries
    offering a bookmobile service. results were analysed and
    summarised under 5 headings: children's services and
    administration; staff; materials; programmes and practices; and
    services beyond the library.
