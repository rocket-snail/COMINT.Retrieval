arl annual salary survey, 1978-1979. this report includes
    tabulations of median and beginning professional salaries for
    budgeted positions in all association of research libraries
    (arl) member libraries, and tables listing average salaries for
    filled positions in arl's university libraries. the tables
    display information on average salaries by position, sex,
    minority group membership, and geographical location, size and
    type of institution. report not available from ntis.
