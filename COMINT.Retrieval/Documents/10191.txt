public libraries in local cooperative systems. deals with
    networking and its implications for member libraries of a
    cooperative system in new york state. after describing briefly
    library developments in the state, discusses the history,
    organisation, and financing of the nassau library system. the
    service programme of the system is outlined, and these
    activities are then related to statewide, regional, bicounty,
    and local activities which have an impact on local library
    service. the problems of new york system development are
    mentioned, and the benefits of participation in a system
    programme are discussed.
