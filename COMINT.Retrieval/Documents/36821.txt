policy implications of data network developments in the
    oecd area. proceedings of a special session of the iccp working
    party, paris, 13-15 sept 78. the objectives of the meeting were
    to constitute a forum for presentation of plans and
    developments in the build-up of data networks in member
    countries; discussion of operational issues; consideration of
    the need for oecd-wide data network policies; and
    identification of specific problems for the solution of which
    the oecd may be the proper setting.
