secondary school library survey. presents the main
    results from a sample survey of libraries in maintained
    secondary schools in england in mar 79. these indicate' a wide
    variation of library provision; 2% had no library at all or no
    library on 1 of their split sites; average facilities were 20
    sqm. floor area and 5.7 study places per 100 pupils; opening
    and staffing hours ranged from zero (4% of schools) to at least
    20 hours/week (44%); over 3/4 employed a teacher with no
    professional library qualifications as librarian; average stock
    was 8.6 books per child; additions in 1978-79 accounted for 7%
    of library stock; and average expenditure on library books was
    *693 per 100 pupils.
