the grenoble inter-regional union catalogue of
    periodicals. description of the system used by the
    interuniversity library to produce the 2nd edition in 4
    volumes, published in 1979, which covers the holdings of 528
    centres in the region. a brief account is given of the origin
    and development of the project. the actual system now used is
    presented with full details of the 2 programmes which operate
    and how they structure the input, and the various products of
    the system. the requirements from new centres joining the
    system are listed, and future plans outlined.
