book selection pressure on school library media
    specialists and teachers. report on a study conducted to
    investigate the impact of warning symbols used in connection
    with book reviews on the selection process. one finding
    indicates that a book review warning symbol system exerts a
    negative choice-avoidance influence upon adult book selectors
    when choosing children's books. results reinforce the need of
    book selectors to read more than one selection aid when
    choosing books.
