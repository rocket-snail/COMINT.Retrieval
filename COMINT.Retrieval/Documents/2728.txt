use of books and libraries give greater possibilities to
    mentally retarded children, young people and adults.
    contribution to an issue devoted to library services for the
    disabled. describes a project which began in may 79 at troms
    county library, norway, on the use of books and library
    together with mentally retarded children, young people and
    adults. the work went on until june 80, and a report has since
    been printed on the work done and experience gained. most of
    the work on the project was carried out at tromso, and the
    practical aspects of it in cooperation with institutions,
    schools and libraries in the town. information about experience
    gained elsewhere in norway was obtained and used as background
    material and as material for correction and support as the work
    progressed.
