preserving our musical heritage. the alexander turnbull
    library is part of new zealand's national library and is also a
    research library. its role in the preservation of the nation's
    musical heritage, an important part of new zealand's social and
    cultural history. outlines the development of the archive of
    new zealand music at the turnbull library; the archive's
    acquisitions policies; its collections; its organisation and
    role within the turnbull library; conservation; and future
    plans.
