the costa rican school library system. explains the
    origin and structure of the system of school libraries and
    resource centres, initiated in 1975 by the ministry of public
    education in costa rica. the operation of the regional
    subsystems is described, showing how the departments of
    assessment and supervision organise technical assistance for
    each individual component institution. the centralised
    nationwide cataloguing programme is outlined, and finally the
    policies of the instructional materials centre, operating at
    regional level, are delineated.
