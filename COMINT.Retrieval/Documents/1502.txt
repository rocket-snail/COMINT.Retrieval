preparing for a survey of all the library processes. one
    lesson of american library training. contribution to a thematic
    issue on library training. summarises the author's experiences
    at the school of library science, kent state university, ohio
    (she is a holder of the gombocz scholarship). to carry out
    effective and economic high-level library work, specialists
    must be prepared to manage general library processes as well.
    us library schools pay special attention to teaching the
    analysis, planning and organisation of such processes.
