central service of audiovisual media in sweden.
    bibliotekstjanst (btj) is the swedish national centre for
    library services and is owned by the swedish library
    association (sab) and the swedish association of local
    authorities . btj works in the bibliographical as well as in
    the technical field. in the bibliographical field the
    cataloguing and the centralised binding services are the most
    important of btj's activities. in the section for centralised
    binding the department for audiovisual media provides 3 types
    of services' talking books, music and filmstrips, slide series,
    language courses etc. in each of these 3 fields the editorial
    staff of btj is supported by a committee of specialists and
    experienced librarians chosen by the swedish library
    association and the swedish association of local
    authorities.
