books on-line: proceedings of a conference organised by
    the working party of libraries and the book trade at book house
    on 12th may 1981. the conference was devoted to the effects of
    new technology on library, publishing, and bookselling
    operations. papers were as follows' introduction to the
    conference, by martyn goff and tim rix; computer technology in
    international academic publishing, by robert maxwell; is there
    anyone in the library not working for the computer? by peter
    lewis; telepublishing, by gordon graham; and how bookselling
    will respond, by julian blackwell. summaries of discussions are
    also included.
