anniversary of the ukrainian centre of medical sciences.
    reviews the history and development of the national scientific
    medical library of the ukrainian ssr, which was founded on 6
    nov 30. initially the library had a stock of 28,000 items and
    served 1,000 users. at present the library has 30,000 users
    and, in addition to the usual library services, also provides a
    translation service. in 1965 the library established an archive
    of medical literature. the library acts as the methodological
    centre for 1,340 medical libraries of ukraine.
