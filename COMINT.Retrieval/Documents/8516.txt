individuals, information, and structure in the
    establishment of oclc' a study of innovation decision making.
    (phd thesis-indiana university.) investigates the conditions
    under which the 1st cooperative on-line bibliographic data base
    in the world-the ohio college library center-was established in
    1967, particularly the decision-making process involved.
    focuses on 3 variables of decision-making' (1) homogeneity or
    heterogeneity of individuals involved; (2) the information on
    which the decisions are based and the information-processing
    techniques involved; and (3) the structural features of
    communication and the organisational setting.
