inter-library comparisons: a report on progress with
    particular reference to public libraries. interlibrary
    comparisons as a management tool are described. a general
    outline of interfirm comparisons as a tool in management is
    followed by a description of the work carried out by the centre
    for interfirm comparison, funded by british library r&d,
    into interlibrary comparisons. the final part describes the
    scope and content of the main comparison so far undertaken for
    public libraries.
