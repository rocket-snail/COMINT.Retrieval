a malaysian national information system (manis). paper
    presented at the third conference of southeast asian
    librarians, jakarta, dec 75. examines the possibilities of
    creating an integrated malaysian national information system
    (manis) in the light of the 12 objectives detailed in the final
    report of the intergovernmental conference on the planning of
    national documentation, library and archives infrastructures
    (paris, 1975-com/md/30).
