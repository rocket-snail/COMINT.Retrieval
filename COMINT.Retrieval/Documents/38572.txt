issues and applications of microcomputers for libraries.
    contribution to an issue devoted to papers presented at the
    canadian library association annual conference, june 81.
    examines the impact of microcomputers on libraries, and argues
    that their introduction represents a critical point for
    information professionals. discusses the applications of
    microcomputers as part of a reference service management
    system, information retrieval systems, and for external on-line
    access.
