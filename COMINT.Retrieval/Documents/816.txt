school book supply) he committee of enquiry into the
    supply of books to schools and colleges (convened by the
    booksellers association and the publishers association) has
    issued its findings. this issue of 2the bookseller1 contains 3
    articles on this subject. school book supply at the crossroads'
    presents the committee's 30 recommendations concerning
    responsibilities for, procedures for, and cost of ordering and
    supplying textbooks and library books. in another article of
    the same title, various aspects of the report are considered in
    detail. in vital links in the school supply chain, baroness
    david (who chaired the committee) outlines the committee's aims
    and conclusions.
