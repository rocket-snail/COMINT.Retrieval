the 1st soviet-american library seminar and us libraries
    (participant's notes). reports on and provides summaries of
    contributions presented at the seminar on 'library services in
    information society' held in washington in may 79. both us and
    ussr papers covered a wide variety of subjects including
    library planning, education and statistics, information needs,
    use of information in the ussr, services to children, national
    libraries and national library systems. visits to libraries and
    information centres, organised for the soviet participants,
    enabled them to see, at 1st hand, the work of us libraries in
    practice.
