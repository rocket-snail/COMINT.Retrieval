copyright at the crossroads. examines some serious
    problems which are seen to be facing the publishing, record
    production, broadcasting, and film making industries in the uk
    because of outdated copyright laws. discusses briefly the 1976
    us copyright law which deals with the new age of advanced
    technology and computer applications. considers some of the
    proposals of the whitford committee on copyright, and argues
    that urgent action is required to bring in a new copyright
    statute in the uk before the situation deteriorates any
    further.
