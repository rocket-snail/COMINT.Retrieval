proposed standards for professional health sciences
    library services in hospitals of new york state. discusses in
    detail proposed standards for professional health sciences
    library services, drawn up by the committee for the promotion
    of hospital library services, western new york library
    resources council, in order to clarify and strengthen existing
    hospital library standards. these standards place equal and
    specific emphasis on' administration; qualifications of library
    staff; continuing education of library staff; requirement for a
    library advisory committee; required library services; required
    library resources; library space requirements; library budget;
    library network and consortium membership; documentation of
    library policy; and continued evaluation of the needs of the
    hospital for library service. outlines the qualifications of a
    hospital library consultant.
