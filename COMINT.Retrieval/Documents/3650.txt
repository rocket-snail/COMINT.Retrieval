which private viewdata system?. report of a 1-day
    seminar, london 2 oct 81. private viewdata systems are compared
    with prestel giving the relative advantages/disadvantages of
    both systems. factors encouraging private viewdata development
    and trends in private viewdata are given along with system
    costs. concludes that the 2 systems are complementary,
    particularly in view of the development of prestel
    gateway.
