data bases: the user viewpoint. an analysis of the
    position in france prepared by a team representing one of the
    increasing number of user groups, covering: the relations
    between the user and service providers, and their organisation;
    user demands, in relation to data base content, means of
    access, and document delivery; and user training. essential
    improvements in all 3 areas are indicated, and the importance
    of user group pressure in obtaining satisfactory services
    emphasised. details of membership, objectives, and activities
    of the existing french user groups are given.
