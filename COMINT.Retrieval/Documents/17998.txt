telidon behavioural research 2' the design of videotex
    tree indexes. 4 reports said to be a first step toward
    developing a set of guidelines for constructing tree-structured
    indexes are presented. they examine the effectiveness of a
    tree-structured index when the existence of information is
    uncertain; the use of tree-structured index which contains 3
    types of design defects; user search performance on a telidon
    information retrieval system; and the use of descriptors and
    the enhancement of single index pages. conclusions are
    summarised and tentative guidelines drawn.
