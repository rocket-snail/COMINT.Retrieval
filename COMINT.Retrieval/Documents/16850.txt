hmso catalogue developments' a personal account.
    discusses some of the developments in hmso cataloguing services
    that have taken place since the author took over as head of
    bibliographical services in 1979. describes briefly'
    improvements that have been made in the timetabling of
    catalogues; the introduction of the daily prestel information
    service; and the computerisation of cataloguing. lists all
    publications currently produced under hmso's bibliographical
    programme, and considers the possibility of its catalogue
    records being made available on-line in the near future.
