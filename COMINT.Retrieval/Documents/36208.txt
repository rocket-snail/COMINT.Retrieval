training non-professional staff' a questionnaire
    approach. describes a questionnaire technique recently used by
    glasgow university library as part of a seminar in reader
    services training for non-professional staff. the questionnaire
    and the discussions prompted by it allowed a considerable
    amount of guidance about the performance of reader services to
    be conveyed in a short time, and in a manner which the trainees
    seemed to find stimulating. briefly analyses the questions
    involved and their possible and acceptable answers, and
    indicates how this technique could be adapted to suit different
    circumstances and requirements for staff training.
