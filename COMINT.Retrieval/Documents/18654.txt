brazilian official serial publications' an acquisition
    strategy. reviews problems in the acquisition of official
    brazilian publications and summarises various bibliographic
    tools available as resources and recent advances in internal
    bibliographic control. a collection development project
    undertaken by the university of illinois library is described,
    including methods of selecting target agencies and the response
    rate, and acquisitions resulting from requests for information
    about official publications' availability. results indicate a
    preponderance of serials among brazilian government
    publications. the implications of such a project for other
    libraries, and particularly for serials acquisition and
    collection development, are noted.
