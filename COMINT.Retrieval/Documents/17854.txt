cooperation among university libraries in
    nigeria-problems, perspectives, prospects: implications for
    national planning. (dls thesis-columbia university.) describes
    a study which aimed to examine the nature and extent of
    cooperative activities among nigerian university libraries
    within the context of historical and educational settings;
    evaluate the role of organisations and institutions which have
    encouraged such cooperation; identify the expressed needs of
    primary users of the libraries; discover the characteristics of
    interlibrary loan activities (the major cooperative activity);
    and investigate the attitudes of librarians and faculty towards
    interlibrary loan.
