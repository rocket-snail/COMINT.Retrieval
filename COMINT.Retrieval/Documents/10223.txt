zzzz. 1st issue of a new periodical published by the
    group for law libraries and documentation centres in the
    netherlands. the 1st issue contains articles on the history of
    the group for law libraries, and legal data banks in the
    netherlands and the usa together with bibliographies of recent
    legal theses from dutch universities and books and articles on
    law librarianship. also included are notices of the group's
    activities and news of members.
