the current stage of inter-departmental library
    cooperation. stages in the development of inter-library
    cooperation in the ussr can be traced over the last 30 years.
    cooperation is of 3 types' within the framework of a
    department; involving libraries from several departments but on
    the same site or in the same economic field; or without any
    such boundaries. the key element is integration' the planned
    combination of resources from different libraries to ensure
    their effective exploitation. this is the basis for the
    formation of a regional library network. in the long-term, such
    integrated systems will have an increasingly important role to
    play in soviet librarianship.
