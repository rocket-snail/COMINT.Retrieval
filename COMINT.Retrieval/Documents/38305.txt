the influence of the network concept on management of
    information systems. discusses the network approach to
    specialised documentation and information centres with
    particular reference to the present situation of agriculture
    information networks in latin american and caribbean countries.
    the introduction of network methods requires a corresponding
    adjustment in administrative functions if the quality of
    service to users is to be maintained. a detailed analysis is
    presented of the 4 documentation functions which have changed
    significantly because of the impact of networking' collection
    development, information analysis, access to documentation
    resources, and evaluation of information services. examines the
    administrative functions which require reconsideration'
    staffing, budgeting, and management of information resources,
    both because of networking and the introduction of modern
    technology in management of information systems.
