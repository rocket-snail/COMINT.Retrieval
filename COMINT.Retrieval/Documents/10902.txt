the french bibliotheque nationale from 1975 to 1981. a
    survey of the bibliotheque nationale's main activities since
    reorganisation in 1975 showing progress to date in carrying out
    its new functions. covers general reorganisation to meet new
    responsibilities; budgeting and staff; reallocation and
    restoration of buildings to house increased holdings, with
    details of provincial ventures; enrichment of collections, with
    details of recent donations and purchases; conservation
    activities and projects; indexing and information work in
    relation to catalogues; general publicity and exhibitions, with
    details of publications and temporary and permanent displays;
    research work in progress; and cooperation with other
    libraries. finally discusses administrative and financial
    obstacles inhibiting a fuller realisation of the bibliotheque
    nationale's role.
