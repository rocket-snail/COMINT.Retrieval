solar and terrestrial relationships the general problem
    of the relation between solar activity on the one hand and
    meteorological geomagnetic and ionospheric phenomena on the
    othr is reviewed with special reference to an account by
    scherhag of events which he interprets as being related to an
    intense solar eruption on february no such flare has been
    reported it is concluded that no clear evidence has yet been
    given of a direct relation between solar phenomena and
    meteorological effects
