the french 2index to official publications1. a notice on
    the publication of the 3 volume index in 1979-80. details are
    given of the contributors and editors involved, and an
    indication of the range of material, serials only, included.
    the contents of each of the 3 volumes, covering respectively
    the publications of the central administration, of local state
    administration, and of public establishments, are outlined,
    with criticism of anomalies in allocation, and special
    reference to the treatment of regional archives. the standard
    format used for each entry is described, together with index
    provisions.
