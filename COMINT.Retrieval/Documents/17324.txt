resource identification workshops and forum for resource
    sharing, 1979. a report to the state council of higher
    education in virginia. describes a series of workshops and a
    forum held to identify and assess existing collections in
    northern virginia institutions (libraries, universities,
    agencies, etc.) in the following subject areas; non-print
    materials, children's literature, consumer education, health
    sciences, library and information science, local history and
    genealogy, maps, oral history, religion, and services to the
    handicapped. report not available from ntis.
