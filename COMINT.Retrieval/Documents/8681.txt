user access to the map3s source emissions inventory. an
    emissions inventory based on data obtained from the national
    emissions data system (neds), the federal power commission
    (fpc), environment canada, and other agencies was compiled by
    the map3s central data coordination at brookhaven national
    laboratory. these data bases are available to interested users
    for interactive scanning or batch retrieval. the emissions
    inventory consists of 2 distinct sections' a point source
    inventory and an area source inventory. the point source
    inventory covers the continental us and canada; information is
    kept at the individual source level. the area source inventory
    covers the continental us; information is kept on a county
    basis. work is in progress to obtain a canadian area source
    inventory based on census divisions.
