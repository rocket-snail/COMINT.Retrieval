the acm periodical bank' a retrospective view. evaluation
    of a cooperative venture planned and carried out in the late
    60s and 70s by 10 mid-west liberal art colleges. their library
    service and central periodical bank worked well until it was
    coopted by the tax-supported systems in minnesota and later
    sold to the illinois north suburban library system, which
    serves a much larger clientele and could thus provide economies
    of scale based on high-volume borrowing and large
    collections.
