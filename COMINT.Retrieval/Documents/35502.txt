information computer communications policy, 2: the usage
    of international data networks in europe. a study of the
    development of international data networks, a phenomena of the
    1970s, and policy issues arising from their use is an in-depth
    investigation of 24 private and 6 public european networks
    commissioned from logica ltd and sponsored by the governments
    of france, germany, netherlands, norway, spain, and sweden.
    discusses reasons for using international data networks,
    describes their technological development, investigates
    transborder applications, presents access control and security
    problems and their solutions, and analyses the costs and
    economics of international networks. report not available from
    ntis.
