as i knew it: the library world. outlines the history of
    the journal the library world (later to become new library
    world) during the years of the author's editorship-1960-71.
    discusses the journal's main features and policy under the
    successive ownership of: clive bingley; andre deutsch; and w.h.
    smith. describes briefly the background to the repurchase of
    the journal by clive bingley in 1971, and the reasons for the
    author's subsequent resignation as editor.
