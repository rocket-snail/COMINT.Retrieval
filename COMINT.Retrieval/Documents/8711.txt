a delphi study of public library goals, innovations, and
    performance measurements. utilising the delphi technique, this
    study identified and ranked public library goals and
    performance measurements, as well as viable library innovations
    likely by the year 2000. the independently elicited statements
    from 2 sets of experts, public library directors and directors
    of ala-accredited graduate library school programmes were
    analysed separately and then compared for similarities and
    differences.
