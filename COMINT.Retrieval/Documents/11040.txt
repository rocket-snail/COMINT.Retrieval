copyright amendement act 1980-report by queensland
    group's sub-committee. in nov 80, the queensland group of the
    australian special libraries section held a series of meetings
    of the effects of the copyright amendment act on special
    libraries. a sub-committee was formed to report and recommend
    on the act. presents the sub-committee's report. its
    conclusions include the following: recent amends to the act
    will seriously disadvantage the clients of australia's special
    libraries; inordinate delays may be placed on supply of
    information to clients; and the act places no restriction on
    the copyright owner or his agent regarding maintenance of
    confidentiality of information gained from the inspection of
    declarations.
