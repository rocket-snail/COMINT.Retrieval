inter-library comparisons. report to the british library
    on a feasibility study, july 1977. study concerned with the
    feasibility of establishing inter-library comparisons. the
    scope of the study included' a series of visits to academic,
    public and special libraries undertaken to familiarise cifc
    with the workings of a sample of libraries; examination of the
    extent to which libraries might be compared by interfirm
    comparison methods; indication of possible measures of library
    performance; and discussion of follow-up activities with the
    blrd and other interested organisations.
