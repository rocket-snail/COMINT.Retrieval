wipo state-of-the-art search program for developing
    countries. since sept 75, the world intellectual property
    organization (wipo) has organised, for developing countries, a
    free state-of-the-art search programme based on the use of
    patent documents as a vehicle for the transfer of technology.
    it started following an agreement of cooperation with the
    austrian government and has recently been augmented by
    agreements with west germany, the ussr, sweden, switzerland,
    and the european patent office. by the end of june 80, 839
    requests had been received, of which 637 had been finalised.
    the volume of requests is steadily growing. discusses the many
    differing features of patent documents and the types of search
    available in the programme.
