photographic archives and photography in archives.
    discusses the problems of photographs included with written
    documentation. cites various examples of how text and
    illustration are joined, and presents a list of 5 categories
    which occur in archives, each requiring a different kind of
    treatment. the conservation problems are examined, and general
    solutions used hitherto discussed. finally classification
    procedures in french departmental and national archives are
    outlined and considered. emphasises the necessity for
    specialist training.
