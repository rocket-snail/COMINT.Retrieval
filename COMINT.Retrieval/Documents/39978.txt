international association of music librarians, cambridge,
    4th august 1980' talk to the public libraries commission.
    discussion, by the music adviser to the disabled living
    foundation, england, of the various ways in which progress has
    been made in ensuring that disabled people of all types of
    handicap have access to music and instruments. sections are
    included on physically handicapped people with hand/arm
    difficulties, walking difficulties, the chairbound, the blind
    and partially sighted; mentally handicapped adults; music in
    hospitals and other centres; the borrowers; training;
    librarians; and other developments.
