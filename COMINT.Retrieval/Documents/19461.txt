interview: alfredo americo hamar. interview with alfredo
    americo hamar, a brazilian professor of library science, chief
    librarian at the university of sao paulo school of engineering,
    and coordinator of bicenge (biblioteca complementar de
    engenharia), who describes the origin and objectives of this
    project, intended to develop and integrate documentation and
    information services in engineering throughout brazil. a
    committee of experts is responsible for its direction, under
    government sponsorship. details are given of the proposed
    structure and objectives, resources, and progress in
    implementation to date.
