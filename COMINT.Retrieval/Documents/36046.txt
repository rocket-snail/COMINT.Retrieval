information science at the french national archives.
    account of progress to date from 1972 on the project to improve
    access to holdings by using automation to produce a new series
    of indexes. details are given of the early manual treatment of
    complex material requiring specialist analysis, and of later
    listings produced by automated techniques after research on new
    methods. examples of individual and corporate lists are given,
    with particular details of the system used in management of
    ministerial documents.
