word processor (hermes wp-6000) and its application to
    library work. a word processor, hermes wp-6000, was installed
    in tokyo metropolitan gerontology center library in feb 80.
    detailed descriptions are provided for the system components,
    which include a crt displayer, disk drive, keyboard, a printer
    and a floppy disk. the library applications of word processors
    are explored in the area of catalogue card production, book
    catalogue production, and current foreign serial record
    maintenance.
