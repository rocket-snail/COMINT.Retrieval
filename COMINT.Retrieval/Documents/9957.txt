epa health and environmental effects data analysis
    system. discusses the development of a system to organise,
    store, retrieve, and correlate data pertaining to chemicals and
    their biological and environmental effects. the particular
    problems of data identification, acquisition, classification,
    and automation are discussed in relation to existing data
    sources and methods of data collection and analysis. the
    problems of computer software development are also addressed,
    and a design overview of the system is presented.
