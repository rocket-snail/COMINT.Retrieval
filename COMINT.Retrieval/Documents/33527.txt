periodicals for school libraries. a selective annotated
    list of about 350 australian and overseas periodicals currently
    available in australia. focuses on titles suitable for school
    students; uses the term 'periodical' loosely to include
    newspapers, bulletins, and newsletters; and covers an extensive
    range of subjects, giving equal value to academic and
    recreational titles and including traditional areas such as
    current affairs as well as less conservative subjects such as
    alternative lifestyles and feminist issues. for each
    periodical, gives title, publisher, cost, size, brief
    description of contents and editorial aims, and an indication
    of the age range for which it is suitable.
