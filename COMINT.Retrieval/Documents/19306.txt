vendor systems and on-line ordering. contribution to the
    lita (american library association. library and information
    technology association) institute. a brief history of the
    development of automated library acquisitions systems, with
    emphasis on vendor-developed systems, is given from the 1960s
    to 1980. definitions for on-line ordering systems and
    acquisitions systems are developed. describes an 'ideal'
    on-line acquisitions system; the model developed is not yet
    fully available. presents a strategy for selecting the best
    system for a particular library, and makes predictions about
    the future of library acquisitions from 1980-84.
