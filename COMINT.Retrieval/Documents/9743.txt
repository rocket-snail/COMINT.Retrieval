studies of chemical literature and changes in the ranking
    of periodicals by citation analysis of data for 1967-76.
    studies the chemical literature from many angles, such as,
    subject-wise and countrywise dispersion of papers, dispersion
    of periodical and non-periodical publications, country-wise
    dispersion of patents, during the 10-year period (1967-76) as
    cited in 2chemical reviews.1 the change in the ranking of
    periodicals from 1967-76 is also studied.
