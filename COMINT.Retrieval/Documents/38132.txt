the long range plan for statewide library development in
    connecticut, 1980-1985. prepared to satisfy requirements of the
    library services and construction act (lcsa) pl 95-123, this
    long-range plan presents library service goals and objectives
    for the connecticut library system for the next 5 years. the
    background of long range planning, the planning process, and
    problems to be overcome are developed within the framework of
    federal guidelines for programmes and expenses. report not
    available from ntis.
